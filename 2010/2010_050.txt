Astronomical Society of the Republic of China 2010 Scientific Assembly (ASROC2010)

Location: Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Academia Sinica (Taida Campus)
Time: May 28 - 30, 2010

2nd circular
The 2010 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of China (ASROC2010) will be held 
at Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics, Academia Sinica (ASIAA) on May 28 - 30, 2010. 
This is the annual event for all astronomers and astrophysicists in Taiwan to demonstrate their 
most recent discoveries and observations, to report their latest research results and to exchange ideas, 
also to organize cooperation when desirable. Oral presentations and conference posters in all areas of astronomy 
and astrophysics are solicited. ASROC2010 is an excellent venue, too, for students, in particular graduate students, 
to present their research finding or progress from their thesis studies. 
Included below please find the tentative program.

Contributed talks of 12+3 minutes in length and conference posters in all areas of astronomy and astrophysics are solicited. 
In addition, an astronomy education Forum and a research town house meeting will be held during the assembly. 
ASROC2010 on-line registration will be available from April 6. 
Participants of the scientific assembly should submit their abstracts and register on-line by May 7. 
The Astronomical Society cordially invites you to participate in this annual event. 
For more details and updated information, please visit the ASROC website at http://www.asroc.org.tw/
