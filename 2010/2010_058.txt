=========== SECOND CIRCULAR ===========

           ASTROPHYSICS OF NEUTRON STARS 2010

        A Conference in honor of M. Ali Alpar

                2 - 6 August 2010
              Cesme, Izmir - Turkey

            http://www.astrons2010.org
  . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

This is the 2nd circular of the ASTRONS* 2010 Conference that
will cover a broad range of topics on neutron star studies, including:

- neutron star structure & dynamics
- spectral properties from multi-wavelength observations
- timing (periodic, quasi-periodic & aperiodic) properties
- neutron star emission properties
- supernovae and supernova remnants
- evolution of different classes of neutron stars
- future prospects

The conference is in honor of M. Ali Alpar who has been making
significant contribution to our understanding of neutron star
interiors and pulsar glitch mechanisms, LMXB â~@~S ms PSR connections,
QPOs and fallback disks.
