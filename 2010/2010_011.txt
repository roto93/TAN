Dear All,

This week there will be 2 colloquium talks. On Tuesday (1/19) Dr. Kimitake Hayasaki from
Hokkaido University will present a talk entitled
 
     "A new approach for probing a massive binary black hole",
    
and on Wednesday (1/20) Dr. Yoshihiro Yamazaki with the title

     "Scaling Properties of Growing Rough Interfaces 
      - Its Application to CMB data from WMAP".

Note that the 2nd talk on Wednesday will start at 3:30pm. Please mark your calendar and
remember to join the events.

Also from tomorrow (1/19) Wei-Hao will be the new chair of the Colloquium committee, so
please inform him if you have any forthcoming visit.   

Lung-Yih
