Dear Sir/Madam,

National Astronomical Research Institute of Thailand (NARIT) is pleased
to
invite you and your organization to participate in the 11th
Asian-Pacific
Regional IAU Meeting 2011 which will be held in Chiang Mai, Thailand
during 26-29 July 2011. The meeting is part of the IAU (International
Astronomical Union) Regional Meetings Series which is held once every
three years to enhance the collaborations among national members in
Asian-Pacific region.

The following activities are included in APRIM 2011
1. Plenary Talks by honourable speakers
2. Invited talks and oral & poster presentations of relevant research
work

3. Astro-Expo 2011 depicting astronomical progress from national
members
in the Asian-Pacific region
4. Public Talks

For further information please visit our website
www.narit.or.th/aprim2011/. Please note that you have to pre-register
online in order to receive the second announcement and other notices on
further activities of APRIM 2011.

Looking forward to seeing you/your colleagues in Chiang Mai, a
sceneric
town in Northern Thailand, intact and safe from the political unrest in
Bangkok where the severity of situation has been gradually relaxed
since.


Sincerely yours,
Local Organizing Committee of APRIM 2011
