The 2011 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of 
China (ASROC2011)

The 1st circular

Venue: National Science and Technology Museum (Kaoshiung City, Taiwan)
Date: May 27 - 29, 2011
Organizing institution: Astronomical Society of the Republic of China
Co-organizing institutions: Astronomical Society of Kaoshiung City
                            National Science and Technology Museum
Sponsor: National Science Council
 
The 2011 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of 
China (ASROC2011) will be held at National Science and Technology Museum 
(NSTM) in Kaoshiung on May 27 â€“ 29 (Fri.-Sun.), 2011. This is the annual 
event for all astronomers and astrophysicists in Taiwan to report their most 
recent discoveries and research results, to exchange ideas, and also to 
incubate collaborations. Oral and poster presentations in all areas of 
astronomy and astrophysics are solicited. All of you are cordially invited to 
attend ASROC2011.
   The meeting will include two plenary talks, one popular-science talk, 
scientific sessions, poster presentation, a business meeting, and an education 
workshop. Election of the society council members for 2011-2014 will be 
conducted during the meeting. You are all welcome to suggest candidate names 
to current ASROC Executive Councilors, who are the same as the program 
committee members listed below, by end February 2011. The ASROC2011 web site 
will be open in mid March 2011 for on-line registration and abstract 
submission. All the contributions in the ASROC2011 meeting, including oral 
talks and poster presentations, will be published in the Journal of Taipei 
Astronomical Museum. Further details will be announced in the second circular. 
Please visit the society web site at http://www.asroc.org.tw/ for more 
information about ASROC.
