Postdoctoral and Faculty Positions in Instrumentation, Observation, Or 
Theory
Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics,
Academia Sinica, Taiwan

The Academia Sinica Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics (ASIAA) has
several openings for postdoctoral and faculty positions. Candidates with
PhD degrees in all areas of theoretical, observational, or instrumental
astronomy are invited to apply.

ASIAA is an institute of Academia Sinica, the national research
organization in Taiwan (http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/).
The institute has approximately 182 staff members (including 34 Faculty,
6 Visiting Scholars,  32 Postdocs, 30 Engineers/Technicians, and 30
Graduate Students).
We seek scientists who actively participate in the Institute's projects
(http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/), in particular:
  (1) optical/IR or radio astronomers,
  (2) theoretical/computational astrophysics, or
  (3) instrumentation (optical/IR/submillimeter).

Applicants should submit, on-line at
https://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/jobopening/, a curriculum vitae, a brief
summary of past research or instrumental experience, a future research
plan, and also arrange three letters of recommendation to:
Dr. Paul Ho, Director, ASIAA, PO Box 23-141, Taipei 10617, Taiwan;
Email: asiaa@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw;
The closing date for postdoctoral applications is *15 Dec 2010*,
and for faculty applications is *31 Jan 2011*.
