Soliciting papers for JTAM 2010

 

As the chief editor, I am pleased to announce that 2010 Journal of Taipei
Astronomical Museum (JTAM) are soliciting papers form the all areas of
astronomy.  JTAM is a Taiwanese astronomical journal which allows local
astronomers or students to publish their research results.  If you have
interesting discoveries in astronomy recently, you are welcome to submit a paper
to JTAM.  Furthermore, if you have oral or poster presentations in 2010 Annual
Meeting of Astronomical Society of ROC, you are encouraged to transform your
presentations to a several pages' article and submit to JTAM.  For the detail of
paper submission, please visit

 

http://www.tam.gov.tw/ct.asp?xItem=134901&CtNode=12651&mp=104051

 

and the deadline is 10/20

 

Yi Chou

Chief Editor of JTAM
