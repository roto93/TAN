Dear Colleagues,

I am glad to bring your attention to the information about the 4th East Asian
Numerical Astrophysics Meeting (EANAM 2010). It is a meeting which is held
biannually in the east-Asia region, focusing on a number of key Astrophysical
processes with the most updated numerical approaches. Taiwan is going to host 
the meeting this year. The conference will be held in Taipei in the ASIAA 
auditorium of the Astro-Math building on the NTU campus during the period Nov.
2-5.
 
The website for the EANAM 2010 meeting is now open for your registration. It
is located at the URL address below.

http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/conference/eanam2010/

You can find other relevant details as well on the website.

Please advertise the meeting to your colleagues and students. We will be
sending out posters to various universities/institutes. Please don't hesitate
to contact us with questions.
 
Sincerely yours,
