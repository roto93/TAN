 On behalf of Prof. Masao Saito, the director of the Nobeyama Radio Observatory,
I am forwarding this information of the proposal call of the Nobeyama 45-m telescope.
The deadline is Dec.1. New 4-beam, 3-mm multi-beam receiver FOREST is offered
for this open use in a shared-risk mode with some limitations. The Nobeyama Radio
Observatory will also support domestic travel and accommodation costs for foreign PIs.
