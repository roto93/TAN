Call for Application for a Tenure-Track Position at Chiba University
Department of Physics, Graduate School of Science, Chiba University

We are inviting applications for a tenure-track position at the rank of
assistant professor for a 5-year term.

1.      Job title, number of position:
Assistant professor (a tenure-track position of a five year term), 1
Tenure review will be held during the five-year term.  A successful member
in the tenure assessment will be promoted to a tenured position at
Department of Physics.

2.      Research and Education Area:
Astrophysics Theory
Graduate School of Science is seeking a young theoretical astrophysicist as
a tenure-track assistant professor (in principle as a tenure-track faculty
provided by Chiba University Tenure-Track System).  The successful
candidate is expected to conduct research and education in astrophysics
theory to have significant achievement and expertise in numerical
simulations.  The appointee will be engaged in education and research in
graduate and undergraduate courses, including general education at Chiba
University.

3.      Starting Date:
At the earliest possible date after July 1, 2015

4.      Qualifications:
PhD degree (or an equivalent) at the time of appointment is required.
It is also required that no more than 7 years have passed since the receipt
of the degree.
Applicants should have a strong record original scientific research in the
area specified above.  Also required is Japanese language proficiency for
classes and administrative work, since commitment to teaching is essential.

5.      Basic salary:
Basic salary is determined in accordance with regulations of Chiba
University and with consideration of qualification, work experience and
achievement.

6.      Application documents:
(1)    Curriculum vitae (4 cm =C3=97 5 cm photo attached, telephone number,
e-mail address)
(2)    List of publications (Description style is arbitrary. List should be
written on A4-size paper and be classified into (a) original research
articles (non-refereed should be listed separately), (b) review articles,
(c) books, (d) presentations at conferences, (e) scientific research
grants, (f) awards).
(3)    Reprints or copies of representative publications, 5 or less.
(4)    Summary of research achievement and research plan. (Approximately 4
pages in A4 paper)
(5)    Teaching experience and ambitions for education. (1 page in A4 paper=
)
(6)    A letter of reference or two references (name, position, address,
phone number and e-mail address) that we can contact.

Submission of (1) - (6) in both print and electronic (MS word or PDF files
saved in a CD-ROM) versions is required. All documents should be written in
English or Japanese. The letter of reference should be sent separately.

10.  Other information:
In the course of selection procedure, those who pass the first step based
on the application documents submitted will be called for an interview at

Japanese speaker and/or person who has some experiences of working in other
institution will be given priority.
