Dear All,

Here we announce the upcoming Crash Course for the Python programming
language to take place on July 6-10, 2015, ASIAA.

http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/school/20150706/index.php

This course is highly recommended by Prof. You-Hua Chu to all astronomers
based in Taiwan, especially to students entering the field of Astronomy and
Astrophysics.

Please note that the lab sessions are *required* for all participants and
based which lunch boxes will be provided.

All participants will need to bring their own laptops meeting requirements
of operating systems with necessary software packages downloaded, installed
and tested prior to the course. Further updates including the
download/installation instructions and minimal requirements for the laptops
will be available in late spring/early summer.  Details of the syllabus may
be revised and updated.
