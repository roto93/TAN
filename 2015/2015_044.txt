Dear Potential ALMA users,

    ALMA will issue its Cycle 3 Proposal Call on March 24. In response =
to that, Taiwan ARC node will hold a ALMA User Workshop jointly with =
tuCASA on March 28 (Saturday).  Below is the announcement:


    ALMA Users Workshop 2015
    Time: March 28, 2015
    Place: Room 1203, Astronomy-Mathematics Building
    URL: http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20150328/index.php
    Registration Deadline: March 21.

    The ALMA Regional Center (Taiwan-ARC) of ASIAA and the UCAT Center =
for ALMA Science Advancement (tuCASA) of the University Consortium of =
ALMA-Taiwan (UCAT) are pleased to host jointly the ALMA User Workshop =
2015 for potential ALMA users in Taiwan.
The call for proposal for ALMA Cycle 3 will be issued on March 24, 2015. =
The ALMA User Workshop 2015 is designed for the preparation of ALMA =
Cycle 3 proposals, and is aimed for graduate students, postdocs, faculty =
members and people who are interested in observing with ALMA.

The programs of the ALMA User Workshop 2015 include:

ALMA progress report and Cycle 3 capabilitiy
ALMA Polarization/Long-baseline observations
Suggestions of ALMA proposal preparation
Observing Tool
Correlator and Splatalogue
CASA simulation
Discussion/hands-on
