Dear Colleagues,

We announce the TIARA Workshop on Astrobiology from June 28 to July 2 to
take place in ASIAA. This workshop features well-known figures such as Jill
Tarter, Bill Schopf, Kensei Kobayashi, Tom Millar, Zita Martins, among
other overseas and domestic researchers who have interests in related
subjects.

The presentations will take the form of tutorials to have a thorough
overview of the individual subfields.

http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20150628/

Detailed program will be available soon by mid-June.

Please also join the Astrobiology Session of Biocomplex2015 on June 26 in
the 1F Auditorium of ASIAA:
http://www.phys.sinica.edu.tw/~biocomplex2015/index.html

There we feature invited speakers: Bill Schopf, Jill Tarter, Kensei
Kobayashi, Leslie Rogers and Sheng-Yuan Liu.
