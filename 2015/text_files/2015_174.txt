Dear Colleagues,

In order to strengthen the communication and  promote the collaboration
among four institutions --- IAA (Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics,
Academic Sinica), SHAO (Shanghai Astronomical Observatory), USTC

are pleased to announce that we are organizing an astrophysics symposium
from December 4-6 in Chongming Island, a beautiful island located about 60
km away from downtown Shanghai.  The topics will include all working fields
of the members of the four institutes:

Active galactic nuclei;
black hole accretion;
cosmology
exoplanetary systems
galaxy dynamics, formation and evolution
star formation
