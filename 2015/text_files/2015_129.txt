Dear Colleagues,

The TIARA Workshop on Astrobiology from June 28 to July 2 will take place
in ASIAA. This workshop features well-known figures such as Jill Tarter,
Bill Schopf, Kensei Kobayashi, Tom Millar, Zita Martins, among other
overseas and domestic researchers (from NCU, NTNU, and NCKU) who have
interests in related subjects.

The presentations will take the form of tutorials to have a thorough
overview of the individual subfields.

http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20150628/

Detailed program is up online. All are welcome.
http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20150628/program.php

Please also join the Astrobiology Session of Biocomplex2015 on June 26 in
the 1F Auditorium of ASIAA:
http://www.phys.sinica.edu.tw/~biocomplex2015/index.html

There we feature invited speakers: Bill Schopf, Jill Tarter, Kensei
Kobayashi, Leslie Rogers and our own Sheng-Yuan Liu.
