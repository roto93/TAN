 Dear ALMA users in Taiwan,

 If you find problems of your delivered ALMA data, you can submit helpdesk tickets
and ask to reset the data propriety period. However, you have to submit the helpdesk
ticket within two months of the data delivery, otherwise full reset of the propriety
period will not be guaranteed. Please read section 8.4.2 and 8.4.3 of the Cycle 3
User Policy document;

https://almascience.nao.ac.jp/documents-and-tools/cycle3/alma-user-policies
