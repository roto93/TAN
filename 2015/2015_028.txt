Dear colleagues,

      The East Asian Young Astronomers Meeting 2015 (EAYAM 2015) is
going to be held at ASIAA in Taipei next week from Feb. 9th (Mon) to
Feb. 12th (Thu). The meeting will feature keynote talks given by Dr.
You-Hua Chu (ASIAA), Dr. Luis Ho (KIAA), Prof. Sung-Chul Yoon (Seoul
National University), and Sze Leung Cheung (IAU Office for Astronomy
Outreach). Nearly 150 participants from China, Japan, Korea, Taiwan,
as well as from Vietnam, Indonesia, and Jordan will attend the event.
The meeting will also have panel discussion sessions about career
development and job opportunities. The full program can be found from
http://www.eacoa.net/event/20150209/program.php

    You are welcome to drop by even if you are not registered to the
meeting. This will be a great occasion to meet with the regional
talents and to link up for future collaborations.

Best,
Sheng-Yuan and Albert on behalf of the organization committee
