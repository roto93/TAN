Dear potential ALMA users,

ALMA will issue its Cycle 5 Proposal Call on March 21, 2017. In response =
to that, the ALMA Regional Center (Taiwan-ARC) of ASIAA and the =
University Consortium of ALMA-Taiwan (UCAT) will hold one day ALMA Users =
Workshop on March 25 (Saturday), 2017. Below is the announcement:

ALMA Users Workshop 2017
Time: March 25, 2017
Place: Room 1203, Astronomy-Mathematics Building, ASIAA
URL: http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20170325/ =
<http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20170325/>
Registration Deadline: March 17, 2017.

The ALMA Cycle 5 Users Workshop is designed for preparing ALMA Cycle 5 =
proposals. It is intended for graduate students, postdocs, faculty =
members and people who are interested in using ALMA.=20

The program of the ALMA Users Workshop includes:

1. ALMA Cycle 5 capability
2. Suggestions of ALMA proposal preparation
3. ALMA science portal
4. Observing Tool (OT)
5. CASA image simulation

Please install the latest version of CASA and OT BEFORE the workshop. =
Feel free to ask ARC members for help if there is any difficulty during =
the installation.

Furthermore, as previous cycles, proposal mock review will be offered =
and will be announced separately.

Best,
