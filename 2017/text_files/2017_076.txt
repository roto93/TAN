Dear colleagues,

The 2018 edition of Communicating Astronomy with the Public (CAP 2018) 
will take place in Fukuoka, Japan from 24 to 28 March 2018, organised by 
the IAU Commission C2, the National Astronomical Observatory of Japan 
(NAOJ) and Fukuoka City.

The Organising Committees invite professionals from science 
communication, informal education, planetariums and science centres, as 
well as professional and amateurs astronomers, journalists, and 
creatives, around the world to register for CAP 2018.

Registration and more information can be found on the Official Website 
here: http://communicatingastronomy.org/cap2018/
Link to the IAU Announcement & PR Image: 
https://www.iau.org/news/announcements/detail/ann17017/

We would like to kindly ask you to share this information with your 
colleagues and to your community.

We are looking forward to continuing to collaborate with you all in the 
upcoming months.
Thank you once again for your support.
