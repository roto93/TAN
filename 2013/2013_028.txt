1. 2013 Asia-Pacific Radio Science Conference (AP-RASC'13):
  abstract submission deadline Feb 28

==============================================================================

Dear all,

The Asia-Pacific Radio Science Conference (AP-RASC) will be held in the
Howard International House in Taipei from September 3 to 7 this year.
The AP-RASC is the Asia-Pacific regional URSI (International Union of Radio
Science) conference held between the URSI General Assemblies and Scientific
Symposia. The objective of the AP-RASC is to review current research trends,
present new discoveries, and make plans for future research and special
projects in all areas of radio science, especially where international
cooperation is desirable, and a particular emphasis is placed on promoting
various research activities in the Asia-Pacific area.

Scientific sessions composed of oral and poster papers will be organized at
this conference in order to cover all scientific activities by URSI
Commissions A-K:

A. Electromagnetic Metrology
B. Fields and Waves
C. Radio Communication Systems and Signal Processing
D. Electronics and Photonics
E. Electromagnetic Environment and Interference
F. Wave Propagation and Remote Sensing
G. Ionospheric Radio and Propagation
H. Waves in Plasmas
J. Radio Astronomy 
K. Electromagnetics in Biology and Medicine 

Commission J (Radio Astronomy) is obviously astronomy related and AP-RASC'13
covers a wide range of topics in radio astronomy including:

J1: Very long baseline interferometry (VLBI)
        Conveners: Xiaoyu Hong, Hideyuki Kobayashi 
J2: Science and technology of square kilometer array (SKA) 
        Conveners: Jongsoo Kim, Tzu-Ching Chang
J3: ALMA project and science
        Conveners: Satoru Iguchi, Ciska Kemper
JC: Digital technology for radio telescope 
        Conveners: John Bunton, Homin Jiang
JD1: Astronomical receivers (I) - cm wavelength
        Conveners: Yuh-Jing Huang, Seog-Tae Han
JD2: Astronomical receivers (II) - mm&sub-mm wavelength (EARx14**)
        Conveners: Sheng-Cai Shi, Ming-Jye Wang

The deadline for submitting a one-page abstract is on Feb 28, 2013;
the author registration deadline is on July 01, 2013, and the early
registration deadline is on August 01, 2013. Please visit:
http://aprasc13.ntu.edu.tw/ for more details.
