          2013 CFHT Users' Meeting Announcement 

I'm pleased to announce that we have launched the registration 
website for the 10th CFHT Users’ Meeting, which is scheduled to be 
held at Campbell River on Vancouver Island, on 6-8 May 2013. You will 
find the first announcement and other information about this meeting 
via CFHT’s homepage or at http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/en/news/UM2013/. 
Important dates include – 

Early Registration & Abstract submission:  9 January 

Registration / Hotel accommodations:  6 March 

CFHT is working closely with HIA and the staff at Painter’s Lodge, 
where the meeting will be held, to provide a comfortable and 
productive setting for our community to come together to discuss 
recent accomplishments and the future of CFHT. Please spread the word 
within your communities and I look forward to seeing you at Campbell 
River next year! 

Doug Simons 
Executive Director 
CFHT
