> *The 4th APCosPA Winter School/Workshop on Cosmology and Particle
> Astrophysics*
> 
> *21 January 2013 - 1 February 2013*
> *Department of Physics, National Taiwan University, Taipei, Taiwan*
>          The pace of the advancement in the area of Cosmology and Particle
> Astrophysics (CosPA) is amazedly fast since the new century. The
> researchers in the Asia Pacific region, including Australia, China, Hawaii,
> Japan, Korea, New Zealand, and Taiwan, have played their due roles in
> making the progresses. To make our marks more noticeable, we have formed
> the Asia Pacific Organization for Cosmology and Particle Astrophysics
> (APCosPA Organization) on 1 January 2011.
> 
>          One beginning was to hold the First APCosPA Winter School in
> January 2010, at National Taiwan University, Taipei. We intend to cover the
> various topics in Cosmology and Particle Astrophysics but mainly toward the
> graduate students and young researchers.
> 
>          Each lecturer would give two to three lectures, with a couple of
> hard problems. And the registered students or young postdocs could enter
> the competitions with the awards given to the first few “students ”.
>            In 2013, we invite, as special APCosPA visitors, Nobel laureate
> George Smoot to visit us while, in 2014, another Nobel Laureate Brian
> Schmidt. Although there may be no overlap with the school period, it is
> interesting to keep that in mind.
