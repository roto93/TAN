Dear all,

It is with great pleasure that I announce the fourth IAA Interstellar and
Circumstellar Medium workshop! A preliminary program is now available:
http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20160809/index.php

All researchers, students and visitors of IAA are welcome to attend.
Registration is not required to attend the talks, but if you want to enjoy
a lunch box, you will have to sign up. Registration closes on 4 August.

The program is preliminary, and we are still inviting additional talks. If
you want to present something relevant to the broad overall topic, please
drop me a line.

I look forward to seeing you at the 4th ICSM workshop!

Thanks!

Ciska Kemper
