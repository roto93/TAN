    Subaru Winter School in NCU 

    Please notice that we will have a Subaru Winter School in NCU on 
Dec 7-9. The workshop is to encourage the usage of Subaru telescope by 
providing the lectures for the data reduction of Subaru data. This 
time three instruments are picked (HSC, MOIRCS, HDS). The targeted 
participants are students, postdocs and junior faculty. The number of 
participants are limited to 20 so if you are interested, please check 
out the website 
http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/school/20161207/index.php 
  The application deadline is Oct 24. Thank you.
