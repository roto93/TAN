Dear CFHT users,

   CFHT is now accepting proposals for the 2017A semester. The
deadline is September 16 2016 23:59 UT. Consult this URL for more
information:

http://cfht.hawaii.edu/en/science/Proposals/

You will find more details on these items and more on the above
mentioned website.
