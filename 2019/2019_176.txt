2nd circular, East-Asia AGN Workshop 2020
27-29 April 2020, Chongqing, China
http://eaagn2020.csp.escience.cn
-------------------

This is the 2nd announcement of "East-Asia AGN Workshop 2020
(EAAGN20)", which will be held on 27-29 April 2020 in
Chongqing University, China. This workshop aims at fostering
close collaboration among AGN and SMBH researchers in both
theoretical and observational fields in East Asia. This
workshop will provide an interesting opportunity to learn the
exciting scientific results on AGN/SMBH achieved by East-Asia
astronomers, and to build up new collaborations among
East-Asia astronomers.

We are pleased to announce the opening of the registration for
EAAGN20 and also the abstract submission. We have reserved a
block of rooms with group discount rates in a hotel that is
close to the workshop place. Please visit our website for the
registration, the abstract submission, and the hotel room
reservation, by the deadline (31st Jan 2020). The registration
fee is 800 RMB, including all lunches and coffee breaks during
3 days, and also one conference dinner. Only cash is
acceptable, which will be paid on-site.

http://eaagn2020.csp.escience.cn
