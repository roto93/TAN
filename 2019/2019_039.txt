Dear Prospective ALMA Users,

        ALMA Cycle 7 call for proposals is now open:


        The ALMA Director, on behalf of the Joint ALMA Observatory and the partner organizations in East Asia, Europe, and North America, is pleased to announce that the ALMA Cycle 7 Call for Proposals  for scientific observations is now OPEN!

ALMA Cycle 7 is currently scheduled from October 2019 to September 2020. Users of any nationality or affiliation are invited to submit proposals before the deadline of 15:00 UT on Wednesday 17 April 2019.

        For more information, please see https://almascience.nao.ac.jp/proposing/call-for-proposals
