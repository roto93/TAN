Subject: [Colloquium] ASIAA/NTU Physics Joint Colloquium This Friday (12/19)


 Dear all,

	This Friday (12/19), we will have an ASIAA/NTU Physics Joint 
  Colloquium - Prof. Robert N. Clayton from University of Chicago will
  give a talk titled "Contributions of Meteorite Studies to Astrophysics".
  In this talk, he will discuss first the nucleosynthetic isotope signatures
  in presolar "stardust" grains, and then consider the isotopic variations 
  in C, N and O isotopes in planetary bodies of the solar system.

	Please note the special TIME (10:30AM) and LOCATION (Room304) of
  this talk. More information about ASIAA colloquium talks can be found at
  the following web url, "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/colloquium.php"

	As usual, please mark your calendar and remember to join!
