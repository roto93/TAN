Call for Observing Proposals 

  --- Lulin 1 m telescope 2003-IV (October to December)

Please be reminded that the deadline for time requests on the LOT for the
2003 IV (October to December) season is 10 September, 2003.  The recently
acquired PI 1300 B camera with 1 MHz readout has been working well for
time-series imaging photometry.  The dual wheel unit can house up to 12
filters.  Currently standard broadband and some specialized narrowband
filters are available.  You are encouraged to contact the NCU staff or Mr.
Hung-Lin Lin, Lulin Manager, <hclin@astro,ncu.edu.tw> for inquiries on the
telescope, the instruments, or accommodation/transportation arrangements.  
The time request form can be downloaded from the NCU/IA home page
(http://www.astro.ncu.edu.tw)

Wen Ping Chen
