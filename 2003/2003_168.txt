Subject: East Asian Young Astronomers Meeting (November 9-12)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
The East Asian Young Astronomers Meeting will be held November 9-12 at
Tien-Lai hotel in Taipei, Taiwan.  For information, see the meeting
website at http://eaya2003.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/.  Registration for this
meeting is closed and no further applications will be considered.  
Interested astronomy students who wish to attend the meeting can take a
bus from Taipei railway station for $110 each way (see
http://www.travelking.com.tw/hotel/02tienlai/introduce.asp?fHotelNO=02tienlai
for details).  Lunch at the hotel is $400 for non-registered participants
and there are no restaurants nearby; you may want to pack a lunch if you
intend to stay all day.  Seating is limitted and first priority will be
given to the registered participants.
