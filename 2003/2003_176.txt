The Institute of Astronomy invites applications for 1-2 tenure track faculty 
positions to begin in the fall of 2004. The appointment may
be made at full, associated or assistant professorship level. The Institute 
currently has 8 full-time faculty members, with research fields
ranging from solar system, variable stars, star clusters, star formation, ISM, 
to galactic dynamics, AGN, clusters of galaxies and
cosmic rays. Successful applicants are expected to commit to teaching at 
graduate and undergraduate levels and should show a track
record of excellent research in astronomy or astrophysics. A good command of 
Mandarin Chinese is preferred, but not essential. In
addition to international collaborations and getting access to large, 
international telescopes, the institute operates its own one-metre
telescope on Lulin mountain elevated to 2860 m in central Taiwan. The 
application should include a vita with publication list, and three
references sent directly to the addressee above. The deadline for applications 
is 1st February, 2004. 
The Institute also welcomes postdocs in various fields of astronomy. Postdoc 
applicants should send a vita and publication list to the
addressee above.
