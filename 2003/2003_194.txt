First Circular

The 2004 Symposium of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taiwan)

Taipei Astronomical Museum, April 24 (Saturday), 2004
-------------------------------------------------------------

As part of the 2004 Annual Meeting of the Chinese Astronomical
Society (Taiwan), the CAST Symposium 2004 will be held in Taipei
Astronomical Museum on April 24 (Saturday), 2004. Papers in all 
fields of astronomy and astrophysics are solicited.

We are pleased to announce that Prof. James Binney (Fellow of 
Merton College, Oxford University), Prof. Rolf Kudritzki 
(Director, Institute of Astronomy, University of Hawaii) and 
Prof. Sun Kwok (Director, Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics,
Academia Sinica) will be our invited speakers in the symposium. 
In addition to plenary sessions, the symposium will be organized 
as several parallel sessions and poster presentation. We 
sincerely invite you to participate. For further details, 
more updated information, and other events of the Annual Meeting,
please visit the society web site at <http://www.cast.org.tw/>.

Registration and Abstract Submission

Participants should register on-line at the symposium web site
<http://www.cast.org.tw/cas93/93meeting(english%20version).htm>
by March 31, 2004. A registration fee of NTD 500 should be paid
on site, upon which the annual membership fee can be waived.
Abstract submission should also be carried out via the symposium
web site by March 31, 2004. The Program Committee will announce
the program by April 15, 2004.
