Dear colleagues,

	You are cordially invited to join the celebration
 of the 10th anniversary of the Academia Sinica Institute
 of Astronomy and Astrophysics (ASIAA).

	Next Wednesday (December 10th), ASIAA will celebrate
 hers 10th birthday. From 10AM in the morning at the Condensed
 Matter Sciences and Physics Department Building in NTU, a
 series of talks will be given by invited guests including
 1964 Nobel Physics Laureate Prof. Charles H. Townes, Prof.
 William J. Welch from UC Berkeley, Prof. Peter Goldreich
 from Caltech/IAS, Prof. Jeremy Mould from NRAO, and Prof.
 Reinhard Genzel from MPE. Posters highlighting recent ASIAA
 research results will also be presented. We sincerely hope
 you will join us as your participation will certainly make
 this event much more joyful and exciting.

	For more detailed information regarding the program
 schedule, please visit our website:

	http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/10anniversary

	Thank you for your attention and wish to see you
 there!
