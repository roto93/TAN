  The Goals of the Taiwan-USA NGI/vBNS Project:
     1.Implement a proper high-speed connection to Internet-2 and
       NGI through vBNS and STAR TAP as the DS3-link between 
       Taiwan and USA is established; 
     2.Gradually upgrade the Taiwan-USA connection as well as the
       domestic TANet backbones in the next 5-years;
     3.Implement new Internet technology on TANet and the Taiwan-
       USA link, for the purpose of faster and smoother dissemination
       on technology expertise and application experience in the
       future;
     4.Devise long-term collaboration mechanism and environment
       upon the upgraded Taiwan-USA link;
     5.Identify potential topics for pilot research projects.

  What is STAR TAP?
     Science, Technology, And Research Transit Access Point that
     connects with the Ameritech Network Access Point (NAP) in Chicago,
     the hub site for high speed linkings among Asia, America and Europe.
     Check http://www.startap.net for the details.

  See you in next NGI express on Oct. 11 1998!
