Dear VLBI user,
 
Last week you receieved a copy of the IERS VLBI Coordinating
Newsletter #1, sent out by Chopo Ma at NASA Goddard. This contained a
lot of useful information, but your attention may have been drawn towards
the following item:
 
> 1) Mark IV Correlator -- Due to technical and funding difficulties, the
> completion of the Mark IV correlators is delayed.  Under the current plan,
> assuming funding hopes materialize and parts availability continues, usable
> but incomplete Mark IV correlators will be delivered in March 1999.
 
Some people may have been led to understand from this statement that
the EVN MkIV correlator at JIVE is delayed until 1999. This is not the
case. The correlator is constructed, as well as funded, in a different
way from the other MkIV correlators. The original statement in the
IERS Newsletter does not cover the planning of correlator construction
at JIVE. At the moment we are aiming to start MkIV correlation late in
1998.
