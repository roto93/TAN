                       EVN/JIVE VLBI SYMPOSIUM No 4.

                 *** DEADLINE FOR REGISTRATION 31/8/98 ***

The fourth EVN/JIVE Symposium and Users Meeting will be held at JIVE in
Dwingeloo, The Netherlands. The dates of the symposium are now fixed:
22 - 24th October 1998.

The Symposium will coincide with the inauguration of the new EVN MkIV
Data Processor at JIVE.

The deadline for registration is 31 August 1998. Please complete the
registration form included *below* and return it by e-mail to the
EVN/JIVE LOC (evn_jive@jive.nfra.nl). The total number of participants
is limited, places and presentations will be allocated on a first-come,
first-served basis.

For full details please consult the symposium web pages at
http://www.nfra.nl/jive/symp/ann.html

Mike Garrett (SOC and LOC Chair).
