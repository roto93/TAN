***       AUSTRALIA TELESCOPE NATIONAL FACILITY       ***

         Applications for observing time are now invited
                 for the following telescopes:

                  Compact Array, Narrabri
                  Parkes Radiotelescope
                  Mopra Antenna, Coonabarabran
          and for VLBI Observations using any or all of the above.

COMPACT ARRAY configurations are: 6C, 1.5B, 1.5C, 750B, 750C & 375.
The 375 array will be confined to the first part of the term (early January). 

PARKES: most standard receivers will be available in the 99JANT term. 

MOPRA: The 3-mm receiver will be removed from the antenna on December 4,
and will probably be re-installed around mid-March 1999. Currently, it is
anticipated that the resurfacing of the Mopra antenna (providing a solid
reflecting surface over the full 22-m diameter) will take place in the
April-May period. The standard 20/13-cm and 6/3-cm receivers, plus
narrow-band 1.3-cm system, will be available throughout the term.
