                                               IAU COLLOQUIUM 172
                          THE IMPACT OF MODERN DYNAMICS IN ASTRONOMY

                                           SECOND ANNOUNCEMENT



        Dear Colleague,

        IAU Colloquium 172 will be held in Namur (Belgium) from July 6 to
July 11 1998. The colloquium is intended to bring together the scientists
who are applying the tools of modern dynamics to the understanding of
various astronomical problems such as stellar and galactic dynamics,
formation and evolution of planetary and satellite systems, planetary
rings, asteroid and comet dynamics, etc.

                          MORE DETAILS ABOUT THE COLLOQUIUM
                               ARE TO BE FOUND ON THE WEB:

                              http://www.fundp.ac.be/~iau172/

        If you have difficulties in consulting this page, let us know at
the internet address: iau172@fundp.ac.be, or at the fax address:
+32-81-724914, or at the ordinary mail address: iau172, departement de
mathematique, FUNDP, 8 rempart de la Vierge, B-5000, Namur, Belgium. We
will send you a second announcement on plain paper.

        You will find below a copy of a registration form (also
accessible on the web). Please fill it in and send it back to us before May
15 if possible. This would help us planning a more confortable and
interesting colloquium.

        Feel free to forward this message and/or the information to any of
your colleagues who may be interested.

        We hope to see you in Namur this summer,

        Jacques  Henrard
