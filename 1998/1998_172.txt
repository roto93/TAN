  What is NGI?
    Next Generation Internet.

  NGI Initiative:
    A U.S. multi-agency Federal research and development program
    to fund and coordinate federal agencies and academia, aiming
    at designing and building the next generation of internet
    technologies, applications and services. The NGI initiative 
    began on October 1, 1997.

  NGI goals:
    1.Conduct R&D in advanced end-to-end networking technologies;
    2.Establish and operate two testbeds for (100 and 1000 times)
      faster speed network;
    3.Conduct R&D in revolutionary and interdisciplinary applications.
