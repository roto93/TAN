The Seventh Conference on Statistical and Condensed Matter Physics

          Time : January 18 - 20, 1999 
          Place: Department of Physics, NCCU



                              First Announcement


The conference, "Statistical and Condensed Matter Physics ", 
is planning for a 2 and half-day workshop to bring
together leading scientists, in the field of Statistical and 
Condensed Matter Physics, to educate a broader
audience about the research frontiers, and to foster collaborations 
among the participants of the conference. 

The conference is open to researchers at all levels. 
Interested participants are encouraged to send an email to
Ms. Jen-Miaw Chen (secretary) at astjmc@ccunix.ccu.edu.tw to 
indicate so. The topic of this year will be focussed on biophysics 
and simulation of material. Conference materials will be 
posted in the second announcement.
