FINAL PROGRAM ON-LINE

193RD MEETING OF THE AMERICAN ASTRONOMICAL SOCIETY
5-9 JANUARY 1999, AUSTIN, TEXAS

The Final Program including abstracts of presentations and a list of 
current registrants for the Austin meeting are available on-line. 
You can access the Program through the AAS Homepage at:
 
         http://www.aas.org/
 
Click on "Meeting Information" and then "Current Meeting."

A paper version of the Final Program will be mailed to all AAS

these rooms.
