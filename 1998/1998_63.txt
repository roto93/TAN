                           THIRD ANNOUNCEMENT

Greetings!  The Symposium is only three months from now.  PLEASE NOTE
THAT APRIL 15 IS THE DEADLINE FOR EARLY REGISTRATION AND PAYMENT,
ABSTRACTS, AND ACCOMMODATION RESERVATION.  The scientific program 
and speakers are given in this announcement.

We encourage you to view our web page at http://cadcwww.hia.nrc.ca/iau190/.
Here you will find detailed information of the meeting venue, travel to 
Victoria, special events we have planned, and accommodation arrangements.
You will also find convenient WWW versions of the abstract submission and
registration forms.  This site will also be updated regularly with details 
of the scientific program if further changes are made.

If you have any questions please don't hesitate to contact us at 
iau190@hia.nrc.ca .

We expect an exciting meeting with many new results and an emerging 
comprehensive multi-wavelength view of the Magellanic Clouds.  We are 
looking forward to seeing you in Victoria!
