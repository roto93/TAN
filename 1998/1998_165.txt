    ARCETRI ASTROPHYSICAL OBSERVATORY
    --------------------------------

    International Post-Doctoral Fellowships
    -------------------------------------

 The Arcetri Astrophysical Observatory(Florence) intends to award two
 post-doctoral fellowships resticted to non-italian citizens. The
 fellows will take part in the observational/theoretical research
 activity of the Arcetri Observatory in one of the following areas:

  1.   Solar Physics and stellar activity
  2.   Physics of the interstellar medium and star formation;
       supernova remnants.
  3.   Galaxies ; active galactic nuclei ; high energy astrophysics.

  Applicants should have a recent doctorate or expect to receive it in
  the coming months with strong previous experience in the relevant 
  area.

  More detailed information about the Arcetri Astrophysical Observatory
  can be found at :  http://www.arcetri.astro.it/Inglese/

  The gross yearly salary will be 30,000,000 Lire (30 Million) with
  an additional allowance of 2,000,000 Lire to cover travel/dislocation
  expenses. The fellowships will be granted for one year, renewable
  for a second (exceptionally a third) year subject to performance and
  availability of funds. The fellowships will not entail social
  benefits or medical insurance but each fellow will be requested to
  cover himself/herself with basic medical insurance. 

  No special application forms are required. The applicants should send
  a curriculum vitae, a list of publications, and arrange for two
  letters of recommendation to be sent to :

	       Dr Marco Salvati
	       Arcetri Astrophysical Observatory
	       Largo E. Fermi 5
	       I-50125 Firenze
	       Italy

	       Fax ++39-055-220039
	       e-mail salvati@arcetri.astro.it

 Applications should arrive in Arcetri not later than September 30,
 1998. The preferred starting date is December 1 1998 but earlier or
 later dates can be considered.
