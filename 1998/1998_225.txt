Dear Observational Astronomer,

Do you need original astronomical imaging or spectroscopic data for your
research? The 1.8-m and 1.2-m telescopes of the Dominion Astrophysical
Observatory at Victoria are available to professional astronomers upon
application.

The telescope capabilities, site characteristics, and application procedure
are described at
  http://www.hia.nrc.ca/facilities/dao/   
The one-page application form is available at:
  http://www.hia.nrc.ca/facilities/dao/observing/forms/forms.html 
The next application deadlines are DECEMBER 1, 1998, and March 1, June 1,
September 1, 1999.

However, you need not travel to Victoria to obtain your observations.
Observing services:
  http://www.hia.nrc.ca/facilities/dao/observing/service/service.html 
are offered by three companies (Aurora Astronomical Services, Seameadow
Astronomical Services, and Spaceguard Consulting) acting as independent
contractors who will obtain observations on your behalf, and transfer all
data to you by ftp or on 4-mm or 8-mm data tape, as you request. The cost
of this service is US$ 35 per hour (Cdn$50 per hour) from dome opening to
dome closing, for single-night projects. You aren't billed for any night
unless useable data are taken. Major projects can be undertaken at
negotiated prices. Please contact any of these companies (as given at the
above website) directly for further information.
