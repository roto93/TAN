     The Department of Electrical Engineering, the Graduate Institute of
Electro-Optical Engineering, and the Graduate Institute of Communication
Engineering at National Taiwan University invite outstanding applicants for
2-3 faculty positions for the academic year starting August 1st,  1999. The
College of Electrical Engineering currently has 68 full-time faculty
members (53 Professors, 12 Associate Professors, and 3 Assistant
Professors) across all areas of electrical engineering. As an indication of
our efforts towards academic excellence, at least two faculty positions
were filled in each of the last several years.

     The deadline for application is January 31, 1999. Interested
applicants may send their resume, publication list, reprints of recent
publications (within 3 years), 2 recommendation letters, and copies of
certificates of education and working experience to:

            Chairperson (or Director)
            Dept. of Electrical Engineering
            (or Graduate Institute of Electro-Optical Engineering
            or Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering)
            National Taiwan University
            Taipei, Taiwan 106
            R.O.C.
