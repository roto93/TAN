          URSI General Assembly - Session J2 -Call for Papers
          --------------------------------------------------


As you may know next year is the URSI  general assembly in Toronto,
Canada  (Aug 13  -  21 1999). Commission  J (Radio  Astronomy) is
traditionally a major  forum for radio  astronomers to  meet.  
In the upcoming general assembly there will be a session entitled.

    'Very High Resolution Imaging in VLBI' Session J2

The main aim of this  session is to concentrate  on recent results  in
both  Space-VLBI and mm-VLBI  (defined  as  frequencies of 43GHz and
upward).

Proposals for presentations at this  session are due by 
** 15th January 1999 **. Contributed  talks will be  approximately 
20-25 minutes  in length, there will also  be  some longer invited
contributions. There will also be the opportunity to present poster
papers. The abstracts must be sent by this date directly by FAX
or by mail to the URSI  Management  committee  in Toronto, full
instructions can  be found at the following URL.
http://www.nrc.ca/confserv/ursi99/call_e.htm  In *addition*  abstracts
should be sent by email to one or both of the conveners for session J2
(John  Conway, jconway@oso.chalmers.se  and Hirax  Hirabayashi,
hirax@vsop.isas.ac.jp), stating if the proposed paper is oral or 
a poster. General details of the URSI  General Assembly
meeting can be found at; http://www.nrc.ca/confserv/ursi99/toc_e2.htm

Full details of all the Commission J  sessions (i.e. J1 - Future Large
Scale  Facilities,  J2 - Very High  Resolution  Imaging In  VLBI, J3 -
Sub-millimetre  Astronomy, J4 - Large Radio  Surveys, J5 - Observatory
Reports,  J6 -  Recent Scientific  Developments)  can also be found 
at; http://www.nrc.ca/confserv/ursi99/sessio_e.htm#commission j
