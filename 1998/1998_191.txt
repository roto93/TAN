Subject: Job Vacancy

The National Radio Astronomy Observatory (NRAO) has the following job
vacancy at its Array Operations Center in Socorro, New Mexico.  

The AIPS++ Project is an international collaboration by a consortium of
radio observatories, managed by the NRAO with the goal of developing a
software package for the processing of radio astronomical data. 
Investigation and development of algorithms to run on parallel computers
is being pursued within the AIPS++ Project by the NRAO and the National
Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA), with funding from the
NCSA.

Classification:	POST-DOCTORAL RESEARCH FELLOWSHIP
Requisition No.:	SO2234

DESCRIPTION:  
Under the supervision of  Dr. T.J. Cornwell, AIPS++ Project Manager, the
post-doctoral fellow conducts research into wide-field imaging by a
proposed space-borne microwave interferometer, including theoretical
analysis, simulation, and development of analysis software. This 
position is funded for two years.

QUALIFICATIONS:  
Applicants must have a Ph.D. in Astronomy or related field prior to
beginning the appointment (typically, within the last four years). 
Experience in the use of interferometric radio telescopes is required. 
Some familiarity with object-oriented methods and C++ would be
advantageous.

Further information is available at the NRAO website address:
http://www.nrao.edu.  Application materials can be sent to: Personnel
Office, NRAO, P.O. Box 0, Socorro, NM 87801 or email to:
tmcbride@nrao.edu.
