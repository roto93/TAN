Hi,

We are looking for a capable person to winter-over with the DASI
CMB experiment. DASI is a 13 element compact interferometer designed
to image the CMB anisotropy over 0.25 to 1.5 degree angular scales.
More information can be found at http://astro.uchicago.edu/dasi/.
We are looking for someone to help finish the assembly/testing
at Chicago, help deploy the telescope at the South Pole
during the Nov 99 - Feb 00 austral summer, and then winter over
until late Oct 00. We hope to find someone who would become a
member of the science team.

If you know of anyone who might be interested, please let us know
and forward this information to them.
