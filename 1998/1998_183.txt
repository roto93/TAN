Commission J Workshop to be held during URSI General Assembly, Toronto.
Title: The Large Millimetre Arrays - Engineering progress and Scientific
prospects.
Organizing committee: R. Booth (Europe), M. Ishiguro (Japan), P. Napier
(US)
Time: 0900 - 1700, Saturday, 14 August, 1999.

With plans for construction of a large millimetre wavelength array
progressing rapidly in Europe, Japan and the United States,
and with discussions of collaboration between the various
projects already underway, the URSI General Assembly offers a valuable 
opportunity for people working on these projects to meet
and discuss issues of common interest. Possible topics for the workshop
will include detailed progress reports on the status of the various
projects, detailed discussions of the major technical challenges for the
arrays including discussion of compatibility issues.

The final topics and agenda will be announced closer to the date of the
workshop. Please plan to attend if you are interested in the millimetre
array projects. Please give suggestions for discussion topics to the
organizers.
