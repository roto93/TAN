The National Radio Astronomy Observatory has the following opening at
its headquarters in Charlottesville, VA:

Systems, Network and Security Administrator 

Under general direction of the senior UNIX system administrator,  will
assume responsibility for system administration on a variety of
platforms, with special emphasis on a rapidly growing network of LINUX
systems.  The position will also assist local users with problems
relating to system operations and third-party software, including
installation of such software where required.  Other duties include:
technical support of software and hardware for local, wide-area and
Intranet networking;  design and enhancement of network services
throughout NRAO; and working with system administrators at other NRAO
sites to co-ordinate computing environments Observatory-wide. In
addition, the individual will play a key role in computer security for
Charlottesville and will participate in NRAO-wide security efforts.

B.S. degree or equivalent and at least three years' experience in UNIX
or LINUX system administration and network support using TCP/IP;
thorough knowledge of Linux, Sun/Solaris, Digital Unix (OSF1), and/or
HP-UX; thorough and demonstrable knowledge of Internet and UNIX security
issues; and familiarity with network programming and utilities in a UNIX
environment, Perl, shell scripts, INN, SMTP, NIS, DNS, Cisco router
configuration, and Kickstart.  Experience with any of the following will
also be an asset: AIX, installation and maintenance of scientific and
astronomical software such as AIPS or IRAF, LAN/WAN design, and frame
relay.  Strong communications skills and proven ability to help users
with a wide range of skills and interests is highly desirable.

NRAO offers competitive salary and excellent fringe benefits.  For more
information about the NRAO, please visit the NRAO web site at
http://www.nrao.edu.

Send resume to Personnel Manager, NRAO, 520 Edgemont Road,
Charlottesville, VA 22903- 2475 or email to: tschlemm@nrao.edu.
