             National CENTER FOR THEORETICAL SCIENCES

                     JUNIOR CENTER SCIENTIST


The National Center for Theoretical Sciences located in National Tsing 
Hua University and National Chiao Tung University, Hsin-Chu, Taiwan, 
Republic of China, has several openings for the newly established 
positions of Junior Center Scientist. Outstanding candidates with 
interests in all areas of theoretical physics are welcome to apply. In 
addition to conducting high quality research, the successful candidate is 
expected to help organize and actively participate in workshops, schools 
and topical programs sponsored by the Center. 

These positions are for two years and renewable for one more year. 
Additional extension is possible for exceptional candidates. The amount 
of salary, depending on the candidate's qualification, is comparable with 
junior faculty in Taiwan's universities. The Center provides financial 
supports for attending international meetings and workshops.

Theoretical physicists who are interested in applying for one of these 
positions should send a curriculum vitae and three letters of references 
to Ms. Hsuan-Feng Kao, National Center for Theoretical Sciences, National 
Tsing Hua University. Hsinchu, Taiwan 300, R. O. C.  ( E-mail: 
cts@phys.nthu.edu.tw )
