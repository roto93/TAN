Paul C. Martin, Chairman of the Board of Trustees,
is pleased to announce that Dr. Riccardo Giacconi,
Director General of the European Southern Observatory
in Garching, Germany, will become the next President
of Associated Universities, Inc. (AUI). Dr. Giacconi
was formerly Director of the Space Telescope Science
Institute in Baltimore, an Associate Director of the
Harvard/Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics, and an
Executive Vice President of American Science and
Engineering, Inc. Among other honors, he has been elected
to the National Academy of Sciences and is the recipient
of the Wolf Prize in physics. The AUI Board is extremely
pleased to have as its new President an individual
with Dr. Giacconi's vast scientific and managerial
experience to oversee the exciting ventures on which
NRAO is embarked and other challenging scientific and
technological opporunities that may arise.

Official press releases by AUI and ESO will be forthcoming.
