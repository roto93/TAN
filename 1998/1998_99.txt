Just a reminder that the National Center for Supercomputing Applications
(NCSA) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) will host
its First Annual Campus Day this Thursday, May 7, 1998 from 9AM to noon at
Beckman Institute. Please register for NCSA Campus Day at
campusday98@ncsa.uiuc.edu

An updated schedule of events is located at:
http://www.ncsa.uiuc.edu/campusrelations/CampusDay/

The day will include demonstrations of applications developed for the 3D
immersive environments of the CAVE=99, the PowerWall=99 and the ImmersaDesk=
=99.
Participants will see demonstrations of a variety of new technologies and
software tools, including a tool called Virtual Director, which allows the
user to manipulate and navigate in the 3D environment, collaborative 3D
visualization tools for astronomy ,and an example of remote design of earth
moving vehicles over a high-speed network. Campus Day will also feature a
number of poster sessions that will highlight various educational and
research tools and applications. UIUC faculty, staff and senior
administrators are encouraged to register for NCSA's Campus Day as soon as
possible.
