	We are pleased to announce that a mirror of the SIMBAD data-
base is now available at the Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory.  
Information on how to access the mirror is available at:

                http://simbad.harvard.edu/

	The SIMBAD database is maintained by the Centre de Donnees 
astronomiques de Strasbourg. The data at SAO are updated every night 
from the main site at the CDS to maintain the mirror site with the 
latest data.  Your old SIMBAD ID/password combination will work on the 
mirror site at SAO as well as on the original site at the CDS.

	Please contact us at simbad@cfa.harvard.edu if you experience 
any problems with the SIMBAD mirror site.  Questions or comments about 
the database contents or functionnalities should be sent to:

                question@simbad.u-strasbg.fr 

                    The SIMBAD USA Team
