 	Tomorrow (Friday), we will have a colloquium presented by Dr.
  Simon Campbell. Coming from Australia, Simon joined IAA only a few
  months ago back in April. His main expertise focuses on low metallicity
  stellar evolution and nucleosynthesis. Tomorrow, he will present his work
