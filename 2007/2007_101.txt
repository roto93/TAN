Dear Colleagues,

  Next Monday-Tuesday (30-31 July), we are going to have
3 visitors from Subaru observatory, Prof. Masa Hayashi
(Subaru director), Prof. Hiroshi Karoji (former Subaru
director) and Prof. Nobuo Arimoto (chairperson of Subaru
SAC), to discuss possible collaborations with NAOJ -- both
on [1] the Hyper Suprime-Cam project and [2] "Subaru
general" issues.

  In the Monday morning, we'll have a series of short
presentations on O/IR research/engineering activities in
Taiwan as in the agenda (attached).
