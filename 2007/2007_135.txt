 	This Friday (10/05), we will have prof. Lin-Ni Hau visiting from
  the Institute of Space Science at NCU. Prof. Hau has been carrying out
  research in space plasma physics and magnetospheric physics. On Friday

  "Interplay Between Theory and Observation in Space Plasma".
