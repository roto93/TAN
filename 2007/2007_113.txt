  Dear all,

	This Thursday (08/16), we will have a colloquium presented by
  Dr. Phan Bao Ngoc. Ngoc was once part of the IAA family as a postdoc;
  he is now visiting IAA for a year from U. of Central Florida. On
  Thursday, he will introduce his recent research with a talk titled
  "Discovery of new nearby late-M and L dwarfs at low Galactic latitude
  and future plan to study the origins and evolution of brown dwarfs
  and extrasolar planets".

	Please notice the special DATE of the talk. Mark your calendar and
  remember to join the event!

  best,

  sheng-yuan
