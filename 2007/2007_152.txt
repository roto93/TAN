Announcement of Opportunity (AO-3) of Suzaku, update

The Japanese-US X-ray Astronomy satellite Suzaku was
launched by ISAS/JAXA on 2005 July 10, and has since
successfully carried out astronomical observations.
We plan to start AO-3 observations in 2008 April, and
we invite submission of observing proposals.

The Suzaku Technical Description which is found from
the proposal information web pages,

http://www.astro.isas.jaxa.jp/suzaku/proposal/ao3/

was updated on October 26, 2007.  And the the Remote
Proposal Submission (RPS) pages at ISAS/JAXA,
which is for proposers from Japan, non-US and non-ESA
countries, is now available.

Due date of Suzaku AO-3 proposals is coming within
a month, 12:00 JST (03:00 UT) on November 30, 2007.

We are looking forward to receiving many good proposals.
