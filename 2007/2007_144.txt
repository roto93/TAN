  Dear all,

 	This week, there will be again two colloquium talks!

 	Dr. France Allard is visiting from Centre de Recherche Astrophysique
  de Lyon, France this week in IAA and next week in TIARA. Dr. Allard is an
  expert in the study of very low mass stars, brown dwarfs, and extrasolar
  planets, particularly their atmospheres. On Wednesday (10/31), she will
  give a talk titled "Extrasolar Planets".

 	On Friday (11/02), Dr. Frank Cheng will be visiting from National
  Cheng Kung University. Dr. Cheng is the head of Plasma and Space Sciences
  Center at NCKU. He will give a talk titled "Observation and Theory of
  Solar Flares" on Friday afternoon.

 	Sorry about this late notice. Please mark your calendar and remember
  to join the events!

TIARA Winter School on Exoplanets in TIARA-NTHU
Date: 21 Jan ~ 25 Jan, 2008
Place: TIARA, 8F, 2nd General Bldg. (第二綜合大樓), Hsinchu, Taiwan
URL: https://www.tiara.sinica.edu.tw/activities/winterschool/2008/

ANNOUNCEMENT:
The aim of this winter school is to provide an expository overview of the 
observational and theoretical understanding of extra-solar planets. The 
tentative lecture topics include (but are not limited to) the origin and 
evolution of planets and planetary systems, planetary interiors, planetary 
atmospheres and climate evolution, planet migration, planet detection 
techniques, habitable zones, and the search for extraterrestrial life. Due 
to resource constraints, the participants are limited to 50 graduate or 
advanced undergraduate students from within Taiwan and internationally. 
The deadline date for the registration is Dec. 31, 2007. For more details 
about registration, the program, and the local information, please visit 
the URL at https://www.tiara.sinica.edu.tw/activities/winterschool/2008/
