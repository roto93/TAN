The Department of Earth Sciences of National Taiwan Normal University
invites applications for a postdoctoral position in radio astronomy
available immediately.  Candidates in areas of astrochemistry, molecular
astrophysics, star formation and comet study will be considered.

The Astronomy group of the department has a close research tie with
Academia Sinica Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics (ASIAA).  NTNU
astronomy faculty currently has access to the SMA, SMT, KP12M and CFHT,
through the ASIAA TAC.  Applicants with expertise in single dish and/or
interferometer observations are encouraged to apply.  The appointment is
for one year initially, but can be renewed up to a maximum of three
years.  The starting date is flexible.

Applicants should submit a cover letter, a current CV, a publication
list, a research plan, and arrange three letters of reference sent
directly to Dr. Yi-Jehng Kuan at the above address.  Consideration of
applications will begin immediately and will continue until the position
is filled.
