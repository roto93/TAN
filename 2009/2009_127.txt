Call for Proposals

1.0-m and 0.4-m Telescopes at Lulin Observatory,
Institute of Astronomy, National Central University

2010A semester (01/Feb/2010 -- 31/Jul/2010)

The information for the observing time request for 1.0-m and 0.4-m
telescopes at Lulin observatory and relevant material are now
available on following web page.

http://s3b.astro.ncu.edu.tw/lulin_2010a/

Please check the web page for the details of the proposal preparation
procedures.

The deadline of the proposal submission is 23:59 10 December 2009
(Taiwan Standard Time).

Major changes from previous semesters include:

 - new form for the cover page of the proposal using LaTeX typesetting
   system,
 - submission of 2 files (cover page in LaTeX format + 2-page
   scientific justification in PDF format) by e-mail,
 - the time allocation committee plans to provide the comments to the
   scientific justification and technical description after the
   proposal selection.
