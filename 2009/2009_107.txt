Subject: Reminder: Call for SMA proposals - 2009B

Dear Colleagues,

This is a reminder that the deadline is September 18
(Friday), 2009, 4AM (morning, Taiwan time) for proposal
submission for the 2009B semester of SMA.
[The deadline corresponds to September 17 (Thursday), 10AM
in Hawaii, USA, and 4PM EDT in Cambridge, USA.]

All Taiwanese SMA proposals must be submitted through the SMA
submission facility (http://sma1.sma.hawaii.edu/proposing.html).
Taiwanese proposers should select "ASIAA" as their destination
TAC in the submission web page.  Please direct your inquiries
(in particular PI eligibility) to TATSUHIKO HASEGAWA
(hasegawa AT asiaa.sinica.edu.tw).

For technical information on the SMA, please visit
    http://sma1.sma.hawaii.edu/proposing.html   .
For Taiwan and ASIAA specific aspects (including the PI
eligibility, the ASIAA proposal selection process, and the
amount of available observing time), please visit
    http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/~SMART/  .

Sincerely,

Tatsuhiko Hasegawa
