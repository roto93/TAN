Dear all,

Next week there will be three colloquium talks. Dr. Marc Kuchner from NASA Goddard Space
Flight Center and Professor Charles Liu from American Museum of Natural History will
stop by IAA next week. Dr. Marc Kuchner's talk is on Monday (7/27) and the title is 

"Reading the Signatures of Extrasolar Planets in Debris Disks".

And on Tuesday (7/28) Professor Charles Liu is going to present a
talk on

"Galaxy Evolution At The Faint End Of The Luminosity Function"

Also on Friday (7/31) Dr. Makoto Inoue, IAA's new Distinguished Research Fellow will
present the first part of a "VLBI in ASIAA" series:  

"Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI) in ASIAA (part I)"

The 2nd talk of the series will be presented by Dr. Keiichi Asada (IAA) the week next. 

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the events.

Lung-Yih
