Announcement

The aim of this winter school is to give an expository overview of the
observation and theoretical understanding of galaxies and stellar
clusters. The tentative lecture topics include (but not limited to)
the galaxies seen in multiple wavelengths, high-z galaxies,  star
formation, dynamics, galaxy and star clusters, and connections to
local and large scales. Due to resource constraints, the participants
are limited to 50 graduate or advanced undergraduate students from
within Taiwan and internationally.
