Dear all,

Professor Masanori Iye from NAOJ is to attend the East-Asian Core Observatory
Association (EACOA) meeting starting at 1:30pm 3/16. Right before the meeting, at 12 pm
he is going to give a special colloquium talk entitled

"High redshift galaxy survey, laser guide star adaptive optics, and extremely large
telescope".

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the event. Participants of this special
colloquium talk can order a lunch box from

https://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/Internal/meeting/SignUp.php

Note that the closing time for ordering lunch boxes is 3/16 10am sharp.
