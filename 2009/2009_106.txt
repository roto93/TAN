Dear All,

There are 2 scheduled colloquium talks this week. Dr. Makoto Miyoshi from
  NAOJ is visiting IAA from 9/7-10. On Tuesday 9/8 he will present a talk on 
 
            "Very Long Baseline Interferometry for Sagittarius A*"

Dr. Yen-Ting Lin from Institute for the Physics and Mathematics of the Universe (IPMU) is also visiting from 9/7 
for 3 weeks. This Friday 9/11 he will give a talk entitled

            "Evolution of galaxies in massive halos"

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the events.

Lung-Yih
