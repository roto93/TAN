                         Second Announcement
                        =====================
  Millimeter and Submillimeter Astronomy at High Angular Resolution

                           June 8-12, 2009
                   Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan

               http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/taipei09/

     This is the second announcement for the conference on "Millimeter and
Submillimeter Astronomy at High Angular Resolution" to be held in June 2009.
The conference is to review the latest finding, obtained through millimeter
and submillimeter observations at high angular resolution, and to outline
the road map guiding us towards the ALMA era.

Information about the items below are updated in this announcement.

* Registration and Abstract Submission
* Accommodations and Reservation
* Important Dates
* Travel Support
* Other Information and Special Notice

1. Registration and Abstract Submission

     The online registration page below has been open since Dec 15th,  
2008.
Abstract submission for oral and poster presentation can also be made
through the registration form in latex format.

     "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/taipei09/registration.php"

     Full registration is only completed when the registration fee is paid.
There is an early registration fee of $150/person (covering abstract book,
coffee/snack breaks, and the conference banquet). Please wire transfer the
NET registration fee, excluding the service/handing fee changed by the banks.
A regular registration fee of $200/person will be applied for registration
later than February 28, 2009. Please refer to the following page for more
details.

     "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/taipei09/payinfo.php"
	
2. Accommodations and Reservation

     The conference is held on the Academia Sinica (AS) main campus, which is
different from the National Taiwan University (NTU) campus where Institute of
Astronomy and Astrophysics (ASIAA) is located.

     The conference LOC will provide assistance in room reservation at group
discount rates at a few choices of lodging services close to one of the above
locations. Please check the page below for more details about their locations,
rates, and reservation procedures.

     "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/taipei09/lodge.php"

3. Important Dates/Deadlines

     February 28, 2009 : End of Early Registration
                         End of Registration for PRC Residences
     March 31, 2009    : End of Abstract Submission
     April 15, 2009    : Program Available
     April 30, 2009    : End of Regular Registration
     May 31, 2009      : Final Announcement

4. Travel Support

     To encourage the participation of younger colleagues, the organizing
committee is expecting to provide some travel support to a limited number
of Ph.D students or postdocs. Details will be announced later through the
conference website.

5. Other Information and Special Notice

     Travel-related and visa information for foreigners can also be found
at the conference website.

     A special notice for PRC residents who are interested in participating
the conference. Due to the need in applying travel permits, please register
no later than February 28, 2009 (regular registration fee can by paid before
April 30, 2009) for required document processing.

6. Questions

   If you have further questions about the conference, please e-mail to
"taipei09loc@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw"
