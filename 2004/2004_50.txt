ASIAA Summer Student Program 2004

(http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/Education/SummerStudents/)

Are you looking for a full-time position this summer working at the frontiers 
of research in astronomy and astrophysics or telescope instrumentation?  
If so, we invite you to apply to the ASIAA Summer Student program, where you 
will have the opportunity to conduct research under the guidance of an ASIAA 
astronomer or physicist.  Opportunity is open for 5 to 10 students, at the 
junior level or higher (although others are encouraged to apply), and 
who are majoring in physics, astronomy, computer science or engineering.
The successful candidate will work at the ASIAA from 1 July to 31 August.  
The salary is NT$20,000 per month.  If requested, accommodations will
be arranged at the National Taiwan University Student Dormitory (NT$150/day),
and the ASIAA will provide half the accommodation costs.  For more details 
about the program, please see <LINK>more details</LINK>.

A brief description of the research projects offered will be posted on
<LINK>project description</LINK> by 2 April.  We strongly encourage applicants
to indicate on their application the project or projects of greatest interest
to them.  To apply, please send your curriculum vitae (including phone numbers
and/or email address), university academic record, a brief statement of
research interests and related experience (if any), and if possible up to two
recommendation letters to:

Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics Academia Sinica
P.O. Box 23-141 Taipei 106, Taiwan, R.O.C.
Tel: 886-2-33652200      Fax: 886-2-23677849
E-mail: summer@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw
Website: http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw

The deadline is 30 April, 2004.  Successful candidates will be notified by 14
May, 2004.
