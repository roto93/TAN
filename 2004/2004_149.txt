Title :   Towards a New Mechanism to Capture the Kuiper Belt Objects

Speaker:  Prof. Ing-Guey Jiang (Central University)

Date:     26 November 2004 (Friday)

Time:     12:10

Place:    Room 620,  Physics Building, NTHU

Abstract: 
  I will first review the observations/theoretical results of planetary 
  systems, particularly discuss the role of discs and finally summarize 
  a mechanism proposed by our group. (See the paper ``The Drag-Induced 
  Resonant Capture for Kuiper Belt Objects'' accepted by MNRAS Letters 
  at http://arxiv.org/abs/astro-ph/0410426.)
