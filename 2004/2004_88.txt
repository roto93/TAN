"International Conference on Physics Education and Frontier Research"
(The Fourth Joint Meeting of Chinese Physicists Worldwide or OCPA4)
will be held on 6-28 to 7-1-2004, jointly organized by Overseas Chinese
Physics Association (OCPA) and Shanghai Jiao Tong university. There will
be at least five sessions on astrophysics and 3-4 sessions on high energy
astrophysics and gravity.  Invited speakers for the astrophysics are
Chung-Pei Ma (plenary session), Ron Taam, Ueli Pen, K.S. Cheng, Sun Kwok,
and many prominent astrophysicists from PRC. Please visit
http://www.ocpa2003.net. The deadline for registration and submitting
abstract is 5-31-2004. To find more information about the astrophysics
sessions, please contact Bei-Lok Hu at hub@physics.umd.edu or Chi Yuan
at yuan@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw.
