Dear astronomers,

We announce "East-Asia AGN Workshop 2019", which will be held on 21-23 =
January 2019 at ASIAA in Taipei Taiwan. This is the 6th meeting of a =
series of workshops focused on the AGN/SMBH field. The East-Asia AGN =
Workshop aims at fostering close collaborations among the scientists in =
East Asia. Given the rich array of research facilities accessible by EA =
astronomers, i.e., ALMA, JCMT, KaVA, Subaru, Gemini, etc, as well as the =
future facilities including TMT and GMT, it becomes more important to =
network and develop research collaborations among EA astronomers. This =
workshop will provide an interesting opportunity to learn the exciting =
scientific results on AGN/SMBH, and to build up new collaborations among =
EA astronomers. Due to the increase of the number of participants, we =
plan to host a three full day workshop in January 2019.

Website: http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/ =
<http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/>
