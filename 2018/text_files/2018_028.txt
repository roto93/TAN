Dear potential ALMA users,

ALMA will issue its Cycle 6 Proposal Call on March 20, 2018. In response to
that, the ALMA Regional Center (Taiwan-ARC) of ASIAA and the University
Consortium of ALMA-Taiwan (UCAT) will hold one day ALMA Users Workshop on
March 26 (Monday), 2018. Below is the announcement:

ALMA Users Workshop 2018
Time: March 26, 2018
Place: Room 1203, Astronomy-Mathematics Building, ASIAA
URL: http://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20180326/index.php
Registration Deadline: March 16, 2018.

The ALMA Cycle 6 Users Workshop is designed for preparing ALMA Cycle 6
proposals. It is intended for graduate students, postdocs, faculty members
and people who are interested in using ALMA.

The program of the ALMA Users Workshop includes:

1. ALMA Cycle 6 capability
2. Suggestions of ALMA proposal preparation
3. ALMA science portal
4. Observing Tool (OT)
5. CASA image simulation

Please install the latest version of CASA and OT BEFORE the workshop. Feel
free to ask ARC members for help if there is any difficulty during the
installation.

Best,
Tien-Hao Hsieh
On behalf of Taiwan ARC node
