We are pleased to announce the opening of the registration for the
"East-Asia AGN Workshop 2019", which will be held on January 21-23,
2019 at ASIAA in Taipei, Taiwan.  The registration will remain open
until November 1, 2018.  We encourage PRC passport holders to register
and contact ASIAA as early as possible, in order to apply for a travel
permit to Taiwan.  We have reserved a block of rooms with a discount
rate in a hotel close to ASIAA.  Please register and make the hotel
reservation early, by yourself.  Please visit our website for the
registration, venue, accommodation, and transportation information:

https://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/ =
<https://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/>

To facilitate more collaboration among EA scientists, the workshop
organizers are inviting key people from major observatories accessible
to EA, to present the status and future plans of the observatories.
At this moment, we have the following confirmed invited speakers:

Kazuhiro Hada (NAOJ, EAVN)
Hiroshi Nagai (NAOJ, ALMA)

Important dates:
  -- 7 June.: 1st circular distribution
  -- 20 Aug.: 2nd circular distribution & opening of registration
  -- 1 Nov.: deadline for registration & abstract submission
  -- early Dec.: 3rd circular & program announcement
  -- early Jan.: final circular distribution
  -- 21-23 Jan.: EAAGN19
