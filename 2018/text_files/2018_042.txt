PHD SCHOLARSHIP WITHIN NATIONAL SCIENCE CENTER PROJECT

Applications are invited for a PhD student scholarship at the Institute
Astronomical Observatory, Faculty of Physics, Adam Mickiewicz
University, Pozna=C5=84, Poland. PhD student will work within the Nationa=
l
Science Center project "Inner Main Belt V-type asteroids as tracers of
differentiated planetesimals" under the supervision of Dr. Dagmara
Oszkiewicz and Prof. Tomasz Kwiatkowski. The main goal of the project is
investigation of formation and evolution of differentiated planetesimals
in the Solar System though a comprehensive study of V-type asteroids.=20

Participation in visitor mode in photometric and spectroscopic observing
campaigns, including extended stays at telescopes in Europe and USA is
expected. The scholarship of 3200 PLN/month (about 750 EUR/month after
tax) is planned for 3 years and starts in the mid of 2018.=20

A candidate for the scholarship should have a master degree in
astronomy, physics or computer science. Experience in photometric and
spectroscopic observations of asteroids, data reduction and knowledge of
programming language python will be an advantage, but is not required.=20

During the course of the project the candidate is expexted to complete
PhD studies.=20

Applications must include:
(1) CV with a list of eventual publications,
(2) letter describing research interests and previous experience,
(3) Master degree diploma including transcript of records
(4) two recommendation letters.=20

Applications should be submitted electronically as one PDF document by
March 31, 2018 via email to dagmara.oszkiewicz@amu.edu.pl . =20

For more information, please contact Dagmara Oszkiewicz.=20
