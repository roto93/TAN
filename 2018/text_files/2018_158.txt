We would like to remind you that the deadline of the registration for
"East-Asia AGN Workshop 2019" is approaching, that is 1st November.
If you need a hotel room during the workshop, we would recommend
participants to stay Howard International House that is located in a
walking distance from the workshop venue. For staying this hotel,
the registered persons should book a room by themselves through
the URL shown in the "local info" page of the workshop website.
We encourage PRC passport holders to register and contact ASIAA
as early as possible, in order to apply for a travel permit to Taiwan.

https://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/ =
<https://events.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/workshop/20190121/>

To facilitate more collaboration among EA scientists, the workshop
organizers are inviting key people from major observatories accessible
to EA, to present the status and future plans of the observatories.
Specifically we have the following invited speakers in this workshop.
