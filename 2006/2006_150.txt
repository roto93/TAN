Title: EAST-ASIA NUMERICAL ASTROPHYSICS MEETING 2006
When:  November 1 - 3 (Wed - Fri), 2006
Where: Korea Astronomy and Space Science Institute (KASI)
       Daejeon, Korea
Homepage: http://sirius.cnu.ac.kr/eanam2006/
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Dear participants of EANAM2006,

1) It is about time to start planning the trip to the meeting. Please
register at
     http://sirius.cnu.ac.kr/eanam2006/reg.php
with arrival and departure dates, if you have NOT done so yet.

The deadline for early registration, oral and poster papers, room
reservation, and financial supports is
     "September 15"
You don't need to fill out the abstract now, and can send it to us
later by email.

2) Please note that there is a group tour to Seoul on "November 4"
(see http://sirius.cnu.ac.kr/eanam2006/miscellany.htm). I strongly
recommend you this trip, if you have not toured Seoul before.

3) We will make room reservations for the duration of the meeting
in Daejeon according to the arrival and departure dates you have filled
in the registration form.

If you plan to stay one or more nights after the group tour, you should
make a reservation at a hotel in Seoul or around Incheon International
Airport (ICN). A few hotels are listed at
     http://sirius.cnu.ac.kr/eanam2006/miscellany.htm
I personally recommend four guest houses in Seoul, but then early
reservation may be required.

4) Once your fight schedule is fixed, please send us the flight arrival
and departure times by email. We will post them at the workshop
homepage, so participants arriving at similar time can travel together
from ICN to Daejeon.

5) Please encourage students and postdocs to attend to the workshop.
A fraction of workshop funds is reserved to support a number of
students and postdocs. Preference will be given to those who present
oral and poster contributions.

For further information or questions, please visit the workshop
homepage or contact
   ryu@canopus.cnu.ac.kr

We look forward to seeing you soon.
Sincerely yours,

Dongsu Ryu
Jongsoo Kim
Jungyeon Cho
