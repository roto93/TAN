Subject: CFHT 2006B open for proposals

Dear colleagues:

   Call for proposals of CFHT 2006B observations

   CFHT semester 2006B (August 2006 ~ January 2007) observation is now open
for proposals. The proposal submission dealine is 2006 March 22 - 24:00 UTC.
Please submit your proposal through the Poopsy system which could be accessed
from the CFHT website http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/ObsInfo/ObservingProposals/.
   The observation time limit for Taiwanses share is 12 nights. The
instruments available in 2006B are MegaPrime, WIRCam, Espadons and AOB. MOS
and Gecko are also available but the scheduling will be very difficult. Please
check also the CFHT website for the details.
   The proposal will be reviewed by external reviewers and TAC will decide the
time allocation by the second week of May. The final time assignment will be
announced by CFHT after the international TAC meeting in late May.

CFHT TAC
