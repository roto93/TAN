Dear all,

 	As you may know, there will many astrophysicists from abroad to
  attend hte OCPA meeting taking place from 6-27 to 6-30 at NTU. To take
  advantage of their presence, IAA is scheduling a series of collquiua and
  lunch talks for them during their stay. Please find below a list of confirmed
  talks to be held in the next couple of weeks.

         Notices/reminders for each individual talks and lunch registration
  will be circulated later. You can also check the following web address
  for more details about the IAA colloquium and lunch talks:
  "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/colloquium.php"
  "http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/lbt.php"

  	Related events during the same period including
 	*) OCPA meeting (http://www.phys.sinica.edu.tw/~ocpa06/)
         *) AMiBA Design Review meeting
 	*) TIARA mini-workshop on compact objects
            (http://www.tiara.sinica.edu.tw/activities/workshop/2006-3/)
  are also noted below.

  best,

  sheng-yuan
