  Dear all,

	This week there will be two colloquium talks:

	Prof. Min Yun from University of Massachusetts will be visiting 
 this week. Prof. Yun's primary research covers galaxies from the local
 to the distant universe.  Tomorrow (08/23 Wednesday), he will give a 
 colloquium talk titled "Galaxy Evolution in Compact Group Environment".
 He will also give a lunch talk on 08/24 (Thursday) at noon.

	Prof. Ji-Lin Zhou from Nanjing University will be visiting these
 two weeks. Prof. Zhou is an expert on solar system dynamics. On Friday 
 (08/25), he will give a colloquium talk titled "In situ formation of 
 giant planet cores in Solar systems".

	Please notice the special Wednesday talk. Mark your calendar and
 remember to join these events!

 Regards,

 sheng-yuan
