  Dear all,

         This Thursday, the VSOP-2 team from Japan, including Dr. Hisashi
  Hirabayashi at ISAS/JAXS and Dr. Makoto Inoue at NAOJ, will be visiting
  for discussions on possible collaborations. VSOP-2, is a satellite program
  following on to the successful VSOP. On Thursday morning, Dr. Hirabayashi
  will present a talk on the VSOP(VLBI Space Observatory Programme) overview
  and the plan for the second generation space-VLBImission VSOP-2. Dr. Inoue
  will further discuss the NAOJ contribution and Asian collaborations in the
  VSOP-2 project.

         Please notice the special time of these talks. Mark your calendar
  and remember to join these talks!

  Regards,

  sheng-yuan
