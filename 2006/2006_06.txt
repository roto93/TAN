To those who intend to submit Suzaku AO-1 proposal,

We apologize for the trouble of ISAS network which caused
the Suzaku remote proposal submission (rps) unaccessible
from all over the world.

The network was recovered now, and we would like to set
the new deadline to

12:00 (Noon) on January 9th, 2006 (JST)

Please notice that the paper submission set (7 sets of
hardcopies) must be also posted by January 9th.

If you have already submitted to rps but have not received
automatic reply from the rps server, or if you cannot
submit proposal by the above new deadline by some reason,
please contact with rpshelp@astro.isas.jaxa.jp.


With best wishes,

      Kazuhisa Mitsuda (Suzaku Project Manager)
      Hideyo Kunieda  (Suzaku Project Scientist)

  Dear all,

 	This week, Dr. Luis Ho from the Carnegie Observatories in Pasadena,
  CA will be visiting from Wednesday through Friday. Dr. Ho has been a very
  active researcher, particularly in the studies of AGNs and Supermassive
  Black Holes. During his short stay, he will present two talks related to
  these topics, one lunch talk on Thursday (01/12) at noon and another
  colloquium talk on Friday (01/13) at 2PM.

  	Notice the special time for the lunch talk. Mark your calendar
  and remember to join these talks!

  Regards,

  sheng-yuan
