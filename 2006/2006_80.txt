--------------------------- ASIAA Colloquium ----------------------
  Speaker : Dr. Nabila Aghanim
  Title   : Some issues about SZ clusters and  extra-galactic
  Time    : 05/05 (Fri)  2:00 PM
  Location: Rm 716
  Abstract:
            Extra-galactic point sources (Infra-Red and radio galaxies)
            contribute together with the Sunyaev-Zel'dovich (SZ) effect
            to the microwave signal at small angular scales. I will start
            by presenting how such a contribution affects the estimate
            of cluster parameters (Compton parameter, radial peculiar
            velocity, temperature). I will then revisit the analysis of
            the excess of CMB power on small scales by adding a possible
            contribution from unremoved extra-galactic point sources to
            the SZ effect usually proposed to account for this excess
            power.
