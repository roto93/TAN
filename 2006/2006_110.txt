Dear colleagues,

As some of you. we will hold a meeting for ALMA-T sub-working group
of circumstellar disks/Solar system. This sub-working group will be
a core group in Taiwan to discuss what kind of science related to
circumstellar disks, planet formation, and Solar system we would
like to push using ALMA. This sub-working group will also have
closer contacts with the corresponding sub-working group in Japan
(i.e., ALMA-J sub-working group of circumstellar disks/Solar system)
to jointly discuss what kind of science we would like to carry out
using ALMA and possible coordination of collaboration between
ALMA-J and ALMA-T.

The meeting will be held on June 23 (Friday) at 10:00 AM
at the Institute of Astronomy & Astrophysics (IAA) Room 716. 
A tentative program of the meeting is attached 
in this email. If you are interested in the meeting,
please join us. In addition, if you would like to join
this sub-working group, let me know, too, so that
your email address will be included in the group mailing addresses.

Cheers,
Nagayoshi
