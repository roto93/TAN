ABSTRACT of "Sounding the Sun":

The interior of the Sun can be 'seen' so far only via the detection
of the elusive neutrinos. And yet, we all have good models of the Sun
in our textbooks. I will briefly describe another way of 'looking into'
the Sun -- helioseismology. Just like a medical doctor who sounds your 
lungs, we listen to the Sun.
