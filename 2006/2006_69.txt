ALMA-TAIWAN TOWN MEETING in Taichung, April 29


ALMA-Taiwan team would like to invite all the CAST members to an ALMA town
meeting, where we will brief the latest status of the ALMA-Taiwan Project.
The meeting will be held on April 29, 2006 at the Splendor Hotel, right next
to the NationalMuseum of Natural Science, Taichung, where the CAST meeting
will be held. 

After the last town meeting at the National Central University in the last
September, there has been several progress with the ALMA-Taiwan Project. At
this meeting, we will brief the latest status of the project, including the
collaboration with ALMA-Japan and the NSC proposal for the collaboration
with ALMA-NA. After the briefing, we will hold a reception to continue our
discussion.

Date and Time: April 29, 2006 6:00 pm   

Venue: Splendor Hotel, 14 Fl. Shanghai Room

 <http://alma.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/activities/CAST/2006/registration/>
Registration on line before April 26, 2006, 5:00pm

For more details, send email to Linda Shao (lshao@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw) or
Paul Ho (pho@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw)
