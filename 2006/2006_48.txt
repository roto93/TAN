                          First Circular

The 2006 Annual Meeting of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taiwan)

National Museum of Natural Science, Taichung
April 29-30 (Saturday and Sunday), 2006
-------------------------------------------------------------

The 2006 Annual Meeting of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taiwan)
will be held in National Museum of Natural Science on April 29-30, 2006.
Papers in all fields of astronomy and astrophysics are solicited.
This is the annual meeting for astronomers and astrophysicists in Taiwan
to report their research and to exchange their ideas.

The two-day annual meeting will include plenary invited talks, scientific 
sessions, poster presentation, amateur activities (organized by the
Astronomy Association of Taichung), and business meetings. More details 
will be announced in future circulars.  Participants should register on-line 
at the CAST 2006 web site <http://www.cast.org.tw/> by March 27, 2006.
A registration fee of NTD 300 should be paid on site.  Abstract submission 
should also be carried out via the meeting web site by March 27, 2006. 
The Program Committee will announce the final program by April 10, 2006.

The Program Committee and the Organizing Committee sincerely
invite you to participate all these activities. For further details and
more updated information of the Annual Meeting, please visit the society
web site at <http://www.cast.org.tw/>.
