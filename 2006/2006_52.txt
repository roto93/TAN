We are going to hold a small workshop entitled
"Radiation and Plasmas Processes in Stellar Astrophysics".
The purpose of this mini workshop is to discuss some special
topics relating to compact objects and star formation, and to
share a common interest in this field with scientist in
Taiwan and different countries.

Date:     March 23-24, 2006

Place:    TIARA, 8F Second General building,
          Department of Physics, National Tsing Hua University
          101, Sec. 2, Kuang Fu Rd., Hsinchu, Taiwan 300

                Faint X-Ray Source Population in the Galactic Center
