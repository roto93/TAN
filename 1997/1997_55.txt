Dear TAN readers,

From this academic year, astronomers from ASIAA and the Institute of
Astronomy/NCU will have a regular meeting every Friday: a joint seminar
will start from 2:30pm and be held at either ASIAA or NCU; details will be
indicated in our seminar announcement.  

The 1st joint seminar will take place at ASIAA on 26th/Sep 1997.
Everyone is welcome.

The seminar organizers are Yong-Ik Byun (byun@tien.phy.ncu.edu.tw) and
Lin-wen Chen (clw@biaa.sinica.edu.tw).
