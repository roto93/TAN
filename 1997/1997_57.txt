	0. Sorry for my short notice.
	1. SMA is one of Taiwan's major astronomical projects in the next few 
	   years; Dr Martin, research fellow of the ASIAA, will review what
	   had been discussed in the project review meeting held 2 weeks ago 
	   in the states.

	   From the speaker:

	       "On 10-11 September in Cambridge, there was a complete 
		project review of the SMA project by SAO's SMA Scientific 
		Advisory Committee.  I'll give a short review of the 
		various talks given at this two day review.
 
		This talk should be open to anybody who is interested.  
		It will be particularly aimed at the scientific and 
		other staff at ASIAA to let them know what is going on 
		with the SMA project."
