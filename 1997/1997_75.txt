Subject: Announcement of an IPAC ISO/CAM Data Reduction Workshop

Dear ISO CAM Observer,

We would like to announce an ISO/CAM Data Reduction Workshop to be
held at IPAC from Tuesday, February 24 through Friday, February 27,
1998.

This workshop is designed to introduce observers to the CAM
instrument, the CAM Interactive Analysis software package and other
ISO/CAM data analysis methods.

Each day of the workshop will include lectures and tutorials by CAM
scientists and hands-on reduction and analysis of your own ISO CAM
data. It will serve as an introduction meant to help users to begin
reducing their data.

The workshop will begin at 9 A.M. Tuesday, February 24 and run through
Friday, February 27. To register for the workshop, please email Nancy
Silbermann at nancys@ipac.caltech.edu. The deadline for registering
for the workshop is December 31, 1997 or until all spaces are filled.
Note that participation is limited to 18 people, so register early. 

We note that CVF data will be discussed, but that the primary focus of
the workshop will be reducing data taken with the primary (LW)
filters. Some aspects of the SW channels are similar to those of the
LW array, but not all. The CAM polarization AOT (CAM05) will not be
discussed at this workshop; observers with CAM polarization data
should seek help with their polarization observations through the SOC.

The technical organizer for the workshop is Ken Ganga.

General organization will be handled by Nancy Silbermann.
