TIME:    10:30am, 06/Mar/1997 Thursday
TITLE:   Catching Massive Stars Young
SPEAKER: Dr Balasubramanyam Ramesh (Raman Research Institute, INDIA)


                       ------  Colloquium  -------

TIME:    10:30am, 08/Mar/1997 Saturday
TITLE:   The Mystery of Planetary Nebulae
SPEAKER: Prof Sun Kwok (=B3=A2=B7s) (The University of Calgary, CANADA)

ABSTRACT: 
  The field of planetary nebulae is a classical subject which has undergone
  a renaissance in recent years. We will review problems on the existence
  of planetary nebulae, on the determinations of their nebular masses,
  distances, luminosities, central star temperatures and masses.  We will
  show how recent radio, mm, infrared, optical, ultraviolet, and x-ray
  observations have led to a new understanding of the origin and evolution
  of planetary nebulae.
