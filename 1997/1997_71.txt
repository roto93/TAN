Faculty, Visiting, and Postdoctoral Positions in Astronomy and Astrophysics

NATIONAL CENTRAL UNIVERSITY
Institute of Astronomy
Chung-Li, Taiwan 320, ROC

Attention: Prof. Wei-Hsin Sun, Director

The Institute of Astronomy announces the possibility of one tenured or
tenure-track facutly position opening to begin in the Fall of 1998.
Appointment as assistant, associate, or full professor is possible, depending
on the qualification of the candidates.  Applicants should have a PhD degree in
astronomy or physics, a clear record of research accomplishment and strong
commitement to teaching at the graduate level.  Researchers from all areas of
astronomy and astrophysics are encouraged to apply.  The Institute currently
has eight faculty members.  Research programs cover theoretical and
observational aspects of solar physics, binary system, ISM, galactic structure
and dynamics, active galaxies, and cosmis rays.  High speed computer cluster
with advanced graphic capability is available through the joint
physics/astronomy computer facility.  An observatory with a 75-cm optical/IR
research telescope is being constructed on Lu-Lin Mountain at 2862m altitude
in central Taiwan.   Applicants should arrange a CV, a list of publication,
a statement of research plan, and three letters of reference to be sent to
the above address.  Applications are also invited for visiting scholar
and postdoctoral positions in observational astronomy and theoretical
astrophysics, at a term of one year with possible renewal for the second
year.  The closing date for all the positions is February 1, 1998.
