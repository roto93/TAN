                       IAU Colloquium No. 171

                 The Low Surface Brightness Universe
         
     Conference to be held at the University of Wales, Cardiff
                        6th - 10th July 1998 

The Department of Physics and Astronomy of the University of Wales, Cardiff
will host IAU Colloquium No. 171, "The Low Surface Brightness Universe"
from 6th - 10th July 1998.

The programme will consist of invited Review and Highlight talks, as
well as contributed and poster presentations.

Talks and poster contributions accepted by the scientific organizing
committee of the conference will be published in the Proceedings, the cost
of which is included in the Registration Fee. 

The Registration Fee will be 95 pounds Sterling (approximately 140 US Dollars,
depending on the current exchange rate). Payment (by VISA/Mastercard/cheque)
will be required before attendance at the conference. Cheques should be made
payable to `University of Wales Cardiff' and should be in Sterling.
