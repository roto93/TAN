               ------  Tea time Informal discussion  -------

We plan to have a series of informal discussions on planetary nebulae with
Prof Sun Kwok during the afternoon tea time. it aims to give an overview
of the frontier research in this field, and some inspiration of possible 
research topics that we can do in the future.  It may take only 30min each
time followed by several Q&A.

Starting from 27/Mar this thursday, at 1:45pm.  the 1st one will be on
(sorry for the short notice)

	" planetary nebulae as extragalactic distance indicators "

the 2nd one is scheduled on 8/Apr Tue. (at 3:30pm tentatively) with topic
on

	" Search and discovery of proto-planetary nebulae "

bring a cup of tea to join us (or get a free one from us)!
