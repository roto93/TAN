                       ------  Colloquium  -------

TIME:    10:30am, 21/Jun/1997 Saturday

PLACE:   Conference Room, ASIAA (Biochemistry Building, 4th floor)  

TITLE:   "Low Temperature X-ray Detectors"  

SPEAKER: Dr Sun-Chong Wang 

ABSTRACT:  

  "The speaker will summarize the advances in thermistor bolometer,
  superconducting granule detector, superconducting tunnel junctions,
  and superconducting transition edge detector. He'll then pick up STJ
  and STE for details. All for high resolution x-ray detection. These
  detectors are actually sensitive to individual photons of UV frequency
  and upwards."
