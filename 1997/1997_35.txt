             The 5th Taipei Astrophysics Workshop

                     
                  Cosmic Microwave Background 
                               &
              Large Scale Structure of the Universe
 

                    Chung-Li, Taiwan, ROC     
                     18-21 December, 1997


We would like to announce that the Fifth Taipei Astrophysics Workshop on 
Cosmology will be held at the National Central University, Chung-Li, Taiwan,
ROC on 18-21 December, 1997. This will be the fifth in a series of workshops 
focusing on current issues in Astronomy and Astrophycics. The TAW5 will
focus on cosmology, with main interests in the cosmic microwave background 
and the large scale structure of the Universe.
