                       ------  Colloquium  -------
 
TIME:    10:30am, 03/May/1997 Saturday
 
TITLE:   "A numerical MHD model for the illustration of the initiation 
          and propagation of a coronal mass ejection (CME)"  
 
SPEAKER: Porf S-T Wu (Center for Space Plasma and Aeronomic Research 
                      / Dep of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, 
                      Univ. of Alabama, Huntsville) 
  
ABSTRACT: 

A numerical MHD model which consists of a streamer and a flux-rope will be 
presented for the study of the initiation and propagation of a CME.  The 
general physical characteristics of a CME based on the observations will 
be briefly summarized.  Those numerical results are verified by a recent 
SOHO/LASCO observed disconnected CME event.
