I'm sending my opinion regarding the receipt of local colloquim notice while I
am thousands of miles away. I think it is a good way to glance at the academic

Subject:  I care

I am surprised that one overseas subscriber said that he/she
couldn't care less about the colloquium speakers in Taiwan.
I am in the US, and I cannot attend the colloquia in Taiwan.
HOWEVER, I do care who the speakers are so that I know what
information the Taiwanese astronomers have been exposed to.
If I have a chance to visit Taiwan again to give a talk about
my research, I would know what the audience might have known
already.  

Yes, I care, and I care a lot what goes on in the astronomy 
community in Taiwan.  I enjoy reading the TAN newsletter,
and it makes me feel at home.  Thank you.

You-Hua
