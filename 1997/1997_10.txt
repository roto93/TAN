Dear All,

The ASIAA (Institute of Astronomy & Astrophysics, Academia Sinica) 
library has some duplicate copies of Astrophysical Journal and 
Astronomical Journal.  We would like to offer these spare journals to 
other libraries/institutes and are hoping to fill the gaps of their 
collection.  No charge at all except that you have to make the 
arrangement of moving the journals from the ASIAA library to your 
destination.

See http://biaa1.biaa.sinica.edu.tw/asiaalib/jlist.html  for the detailed 
list of available journals, and send your request/enquiry to 

clw@biaa.sinica.edu.tw or sandra@biaa.sinica.edu.tw 

Our offers will be made later depending on how many requests we receive.

Happy new year,

ASIAA Library
