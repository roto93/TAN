2. Radio Astronomy news from WRC '97 is available on Willem Baan's
   IUCAF site at NAIC: http://www.naic.edu/iucaf/iucaf.html. See
   IUCAF documents dated 1997 November. This is wakeup call to 
   continue strenuous effort in preserving the spectrum "natural 
   wilderness" for radio astronomy; see Willem's paragraph about 
   about lavish parties and receptions hosted by spectrum-deprived, 
   commercial wanna-be's. Also, see list of conference trends
   with items about "peaceful coexistence" (reminds me of my 
   cold warrior era youth); "mmwave spectrum" with implicit need
   for more involvement of mm astronomers in spectrum management
   issues; and "new allies" Earth Exploration Satellite (EES)
   community. Don't be put off by alphabet soup of WRC/IUCAF/...
   as this activity is extremely important to the future of our
   science.
