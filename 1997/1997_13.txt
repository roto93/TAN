PS: Dr. Wilson will also give a talk at Rm C402, Earth Sciences Dept.,
    Taiwan Normal Univ. at 2 pm on February 25th (Tuesday):

        Title: The Cold Universe of Molecular Clouds
     Abstract: This is a historical development from 1965 to the present,
               showing how our understanding of molecular clouds has
               changed with time. The emphasis will be on molecular
               clouds in our galaxy, and will include a short account
               of the relation of molecular clouds and star formation,
               interstellar chemistry, galactic dynamics and cosmology.
