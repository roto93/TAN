(2nd Circular, Sep/1997)
 

             The 5th Taipei Astrophysics Workshop
 
                     
                  Cosmic Microwave Background 
                               &
              Large Scale Structure of the Universe
 
 
                    Chung-Li, Taiwan, ROC     
                     18-21 December, 1997
 
 
The Fifth Taipei Astrophysics Workshop will be held at the National
Central University, Chung-Li, Taiwan, ROC on 18-21 December, 1997. This
will be the fifth in a series of workshops focusing on current issues in
Astronomy and Astrophycics. The TAW5 will focus on cosmology, with main
interests in the cosmic microwave background and the large scale structure
of the Universe.

Topics include:
=============== 
Cosmic microwave background - anisotropy, polarization, SZ effect
Large scale structures - N-body simulations, clusters, deep redshift surveys
Dark matter - MACHO, axion, SUSY particles, neutrino
Electroweak baryogenesis - antimatter search, CP violation, phase transition
Weak and micro gravitational lensing
