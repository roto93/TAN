Dear All,



The 2014 International School for Young Astronomers (ISYA) will be held in
Chiang Mai, Thailand from November 24 to December 12. It is jointly
organized by the National Astronomical Research Institute of Thailand
(NARIT), Chiang Mai University (CMU) and by the Yunnan Observatories (YNO) of
the Chinese Academy of Sciences with fully sponsored by the International
Astronomical Union (IAU).



Students are invited to submit their applications from this moment until
August 8, 2014.

More details can be found on the web site http://www.narit.or.th/en/isya.



Please distribute this information to potential applicants.
