The CFHT 2014B Call for Proposals is now open.

Information for Phase 1 can be found at:
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/en/science/Proposals/

Instrument-specific tutorials and information are available:

MegaCam:
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/en/science/QSO/ph1_MC.html

WIRCam:
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/en/science/QSO/ph1_WC.html
For WIRCam, pay careful attention to the added note about narrow band filters
and their availability and about the staring mode limitations!

ESPaDOnS:
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/en/science/QSO/ph1_ESP.html

PUEO:
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/Instruments/Imaging/AOB/

The deadline is: Thursday March 20th at 23:59 UTC

All users are encouraged to read the documentation available (QSO
tutorials, instrument tutorials, and NorthStar help files).

Daniel Devost on behalf of CFHT.
