The Astronomical Society of the Republic of China, ASROC, has her new Council
members elected in the 2014 General Assembly held recently in Sitou on May 25.

The 25th Council of ASROC consists of 

  President: You-Hua CHU
  Secretary General: Shiang-Yu WANG
  Executive Council members: Shih-Ping LAI and Sheng-Yuan LIU
  Members of Council: Linwen CHEN, Vivien CHEN, Yi-Nan CHIN, Daisuke KINOSHITA,
                      Albert KONG, Li-Hwai LIN
  Managing Supervisor: Chorng-Yuan HWANG
  Members of Supervisory Board: Hsiang-Kuang CHANG and Yi-Jehng KUAN
