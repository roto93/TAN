Osaka University offers Master and Ph.D. programs in various
disciplines, including:

Biotechnology,
Civil Engineering,
Physics,
Chemistry,
Biology,
Computer Science and Technology.

Education and research is conducted in English.

Most international students receive a scholarship.

For details and applications, see the webpage below.
Applications deadlines are different for the different programs,
but they are mostly in November/December 2014, for enrolment in Osaka
University in October 2015.

http://caren.eng.osaka-u.ac.jp/programs-science/

- Flyer with more information:
http://kakudan.rcnp.osaka-u.ac.jp/~baiotti/CAREN/OsakaU-Study-in-English-pamphlet_2014.pdf
