> East-Asian Workshop
> "Evolution of Molecular Clouds: Recent Observational and Theoretical
> Results and Prospects"
>
> ---Second Announcement---
>
> Dates: 2006 February 6th 13:00 to 8th 16:00
> Location: National Astronomical Observatory, Mitaka, Tokyo, Japan
>
> The East-Asian Workshop on "Evolution of Molecular Clouds: Recent
> Observational and Theoretical Results and Prospects" will be held in
> Mitaka, Tokyo, Japan.
> The purpose of this meeting is to bring researchers in East Asian
> regions (i.e. China, Korea, Taiwan, and Japan) together and exchange
> the recent observational and theoretical progresses in the evolution of
> molecular clouds and related topics.
>
> The scientific programs will cover the following aspects:
> - internal structure of molecular cloud cores
> - turbulence in molecular clouds
> - interaction of supernova remnants and molecular clouds
> - molecular gas traced by submillimeter lines
> - physical condition and evolution of protostars
> - binary and multiple star formation
> - formation of stellar clusters
> etc.
>
> We hope this workshop would help us to improve collaboration in the
> East Asian researchers and help us to develop better scientific ideas
> that can lead the age of ALMA.
