  Dear all,

 	Next week, we will have another colloquium at a special time.
  Dr. Yuk-Tung Liu, the visiting speaker, currently works with the GR
  numerical group lead by Prof. Shapiro at University of Illinois at
  Urbana-Champaign, and is an expert on relativistic MHD simulations.
  Next Tuesday afternoon, he  will give a talk titled "General relativistic
  hydrodynamics with  viscosity : contraction, catastrophic collapse,
  and disk formation in hypermassive neutron stars".

 	Please mark your calendar and remember to join!

  Regards,

  sheng-yuan
