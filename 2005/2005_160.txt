  Dear all,

 	This Friday, we will have a colloquium presented by Prof. Yong-Ik
  Byun from Yonsei University, now on an academic visit at NCU this year.
  On Friday, he will introduce the GALEX mission, and particularly the
  science highlights related to his research on the evolution of elliptical
  galaxies.

 	Please mark your calendar and remember to join!

  Regards,

  sheng-yuan

  ------------------------- ASIAA Colloquium -----------------------
   Speaker : Prof. Yong-Ik Byun
               (Yonsei University/National Central University)
   Title   : Ultraviolet Space Telescope GALEX
   Time    : 11/18 (Fri) 2:00 PM
   Location: ASIAA Room 716
   Abstract:
             Center for Space Astrophysics at Yonsei University has been an
             active partner for the GALEX mission.   Brief introduction and
             science highlights of the mission will be presented with
             particular emphasis on the evolution of elliptical galaxies
             which is our primary area of research with GALEX observations.
