The dealine for proposals for semester 2006A of the CFHT is 2005 Sep 21 - 
24:00 UTC. Please submit your proposal online. Details of the procedure 
can be found in the CFHT web site: 
http://www.cfht.hawaii.edu/ObsInfo/ObservingProposals/.

Please note in particular the new instruments available include WIRCAM and 
ESPaDOnS.

All proposals for the Taiwanese share of time will be externally refereed.
