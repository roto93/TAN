------
                           Second Announcement

                    ASIAA/TIARA Star Formation Workshop
                    "From Cloud Cores to Debris Disks"
                             Taipei, Taiwan
                          11-14 December 2005

            http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/sf2005/workshop/

   The theme this year is "From Cloud Cores to Debris Disks", which will  
have
   a broad coverage of early stages of star formation at different  
wavelengths
   from accessible instruments around the globe. Format of talks and  
discussions
   will follow the annual workshop sponsored by the Center for Star  
Formation
   Studies in the past.  All past participants of previous workshops and  
new
   star formation researchers and students are welcome.

REGISTRATION
------------

   We have so far about 60 registrations, and there's room for more  
participants
   (up to 100)! We sincerely encourage you to join by registering soon!  
To
   register, please fill out the web form at the following link,
          http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/sf2005/registration/open/

   Please be reminded that the deadline for registration is Oct 31th,  
2005!

VISA
----

   For registered participants, please do check the following web-link  
(also
   accessible via the workshop web-page) regarding the visa regulation
   applicable to your nationality.
          http://www.boca.gov.tw/%7Eboca2000/en/exempte.htm

   Please contact us should you need our assistance in your visa  
application
   regarding documents for verifying the purpose of your visit.

EXCURSION
---------

   There are still plenty of slots available for the excursion  
immediately
   after the workshop on Dec 15-16!

   At the excursion, the participants and guests will join a two-day  
one-night
   (Dec 15-16) trip to the eastern shore of Taiwan. We will first take a  
train
   ride in the morning of Dec 15th from Taipei, and check into a newly  
opened
   and very popular resort in Chao-Hsi in Yilan County. The scenic  
location
   provides an overlook of the pacific in the day, and the glamorous  
view of
   Yilan at night. During the stay, you can hiking around the waterfall  
at the
   scenic Wufongci (five flag-like peaks) area as well as enjoying the  
famous
   Chao-Hsi hot spring and spa - a perfect relaxation for December time  
in
   Taipei. In the afternoon on Dec 16th, we will return to Taipei by  
train
   again.

   Sign up soon if you are interested!
