          Inaugural Japan-Taiwan ALMA Science Workshop

              ****   2nd Announcement   ***

The ASIAA will host the inaugural ALMA Japan-Taiwan (ALMA-JT) scientific
workshop on 15-16 December 2005.  The purpose of this announcement is to
provide information on the current list of invited speakers, encourage
students to participate and present oral talks, urge attendees who require
accomodations to reserve their accomodations as soon as is possible, and
remind everyone to register and provide titles and abstracts by 13 November
2005, including *all invited speakers*.

Invited Speakers
----------------

The SOC is putting together a list of invited speakers, and expect to select
more from the registered abstracts.   We anticipate scheduling 30 mins for
review talks, and 20 mins for invited talks.  The confirmed speakers are

Students
--------
We strongly encourage students, particularly from Taiwan, to attend,
participate in the discussions, and present talks.  We anticipate scheduling
12 mins for student contributed talks, and will make the selection from the
registered abstracts.

Accomodations
-------------
We strongly encourage participants who require accomodations to reserve
early so as to obtain the type of room you would like.

Registration
------------
The dateline for registration is 13 November 2005.  Please provide a
title and abstract of your suggested oral contribution, as well as
any accomodation needs, at

http://alma.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/activities/workshop2005

All invited speakers are required to register and provide a
title and abstract.
