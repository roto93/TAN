  Dear all,

  	This week we have a visitor, Dr. Ramon Brasser from Queen's
  University at Canada. Dr. Brasser has been working of the formation
  and dynamics of the Solar System via computer simulations. On Friday,
  he will give a colloquium titled "Embedded Star Clusters and the
  Formation of the Inner Oort Cloud".

  	Please mark your calendar for this talk and remember to join!

  Regards,

  sheng-yuan
