CALL FOR OBSERVING PROPOSALS
The Lulin One-meter Telescope (LOT) calls for observing time requests for the 
2005B semester which runs from July 1 to December 31.  In addition to the set 
of Bessel BVRI filters, some specialized comet and nebulosity filters are 
also available.  A focal reducer to change from f/8 to f/4, thereby doubling 
the FOV, is ready for the semester. Aside from scientific programs, we may 
also  consider  "education" projects for advanced observing classes.  The 
observing proposal form can be downloaded from the NCU/IA homepage 
(http://www.astro.ncu.edu.tw).   The deadline is  June 1, 2005.
