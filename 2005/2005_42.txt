  ------------------------ ASIAA Colloquium ------------------------
   Speaker : Dr. Pascal Puget (Observatory of Paris in Meudon)
   Title   : From NAOS to Planet Finder : The AO System of VLT
   Time    : 04/04 (Mon) 2:00 PM
   Location: ASIAA Room 716
   Abstract: In this talk, Dr. Puget will give a review about the first
            AO system of VLT (NAOS) and some of the scientific results
             with this system. He will then introduce the new AO system
             proposal "planet finder". The new system is designated for
             extrasolar planet detection. The system design concept,
             scientific requirement and system feasibility will be
             presented.
