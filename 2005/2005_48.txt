                     ASIAA Summer Student Program 2005

  ------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Are you looking for a full-time position this summer working at the
  frontiers of research in astronomy and astrophysics or telescope
  instrumentation?
  If so, we invite you to apply to the ASIAA Summer Student program,
  where you will have the opportunity to conduct research under the
  guidance of an ASIAA astronomer or physicist. Opportunity is open up to
  about 10 students with preference given to the junior level or higher,
  and who are majoring in physics, astronomy, computer science or
  engineering. The successful candidate will work at the ASIAA from 1
  July to 31 August. The salary is NT$20,000 per month. If requested,
  accommodations will be arranged at the National Taiwan University
  Student Dormitory (NT$150/day), and the ASIAA will provide half the
  accommodation costs. Here is more details about the program.

         A brief description of the research projects offered will be
         posted on project description by 5 April. We strongly encourage
         applicants to indicate on their application the project or
         projects of greatest interest to them. To apply, please use our
         application form on-line, send your university academic record,
         and if possible up to two recommendation letters to:

         Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics Academia Sinica
         P.O. Box 23-141 Taipei 106, Taiwan, R.O.C.
         or, E-mail: summer@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw

         Tel: 886-2-33652200      Fax: 886-2-23677849
         ASIAA Website: http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/
         Program site: http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/Education/SummerStudents/

         The dead line is 30 April, 2005.
         Successful candidates will be notified by 16 May, 2005.
