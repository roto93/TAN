5th Italian-Sino Workshop on Relativistic Astrophysics 
-- Lights of the Dark Universe 

May 28 - June 1, 2008, Taipei-Hualien, Taiwan 

The Workshop will be held at the Academia Sinica (Taipei) on 
May 28 - May 29 and then continued at the National Dong Hwa 
University (Hualien) on May 30 - June 1.           

Topics: dark energy, large scale structures, gamma ray bursts, 
        supernovae, first stars 

Preliminary agenda:
5/28  Invited talks AS
5/29  Invited talks AS
5/30  Excursion to Taroku National Park, Hualien & Banquet
5/31  Invited talks NDHU
6/1   Invited talks NDHU, ends at noon
