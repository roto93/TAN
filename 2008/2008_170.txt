Dear all,

Professor Masahiro Takada of Institute of the Physics and Mathematics of the Universe,
Tokyo University is visiting us next week from 11/17-21. His research interest is in
observational cosmology: the nature of dark energy and dark matter, weak lensing due to
large-scale structure, and galaxy clustering statistics. And next Tuesday he is going to
 present a talk titled

"Neutrino Mass and Cosmology"

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the event. 

Lung-Yih
