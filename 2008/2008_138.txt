Dear all,

There will be 2 colloquia this week. Tomorrow (9/16) ALMA Project Scientist Professor
Richard Hills is give a talk about

"ALMA - Its Current Statue and Future Promise".

Please note that the talk will start at 1:30pm. 

Dr. Ming-Chang Liu recently received his PhD from UCLA and is currently an IAA post-doc
based at Carnegie Institute of Washington. On Friday (9/19) he will present a talk titled

"Short-lived radionuclides, early solar system chronology and chemical evolution"

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the events.

Lung-Yih
