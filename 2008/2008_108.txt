Dear all,

Dr. Anita Richards of University of Manchester is visiting us this week. Dr. Richards 
is a support scientist of Multi-Element Radio Linked Interferometer Network (MERLIN), 
an array of radio telescopes distributed around UK with separations of up to 217km. 
She is also an expert on masers in red giants and redsupergiants and has conducted 
many observations with the MERLIN array. This Friday (7/11) she is presenting a talk 
titled 

"MERLIN/VLBI maser measurements of M-stars' mass loss"

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the event.

Lung-Yih
