Second Announcement on 
The 5th Japan-Taiwan ALMA Science Workshop 
"Star Formation and Astrochemistry" 
                    5-6 December 2008, 
             National Tsing-Hua University, Taiwan 
http://www2.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/act/alma/200812/index.php 

Registration for oral presentations has ended on Nov 7 and the 
tentative schedule has been posted on our web page.  The SOC would 
like to encourage more submissions with poster presentations and 
without presentations.  The deadlines will be Nov 20th. 

Those who register for oral presentations should fill out the form for 
ALMA Design Reference Science Plan (DRSP, see 
http://www.strw.leidenuniv.nl/~alma/drsp2_template.txt) and submit it 
to the SOC (almajt5 asiaa.sinica.edu.tw) by the end of November. 
Information on ALMA sensitivity can be found at 
http://www.cv.nrao.edu/naasc/alma_sensitivity.shtml.  All participants 
will receive the printout of these DRSP in the beginning of the 
meeting in order to facilitate the discussion during the workwhop. 

The hotel arrangement will be posted in our web page shortly after the 
deadline of the registration. Our web page also provides travel 
information to NTHU along with various maps. If you have any 
additional questions, please send your questions to almajt5 
asiaa.sinica.edu.tw.
