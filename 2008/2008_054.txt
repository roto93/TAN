Dear all,

This Friday we will have a colloquium talk presented by Dr. Jiwoo Nam, 
who is currently a visiting scholar of CosPA at NTU. Dr. Nam has been 
involved with Antarctic Impulsive Transient Antenna (ANITA), a 
balloon-borne neutrino telescope search for high energy neutrinos 
created by collisions between cosmic rays and the the cosmic microwave 
photons that pervade the Universe. And the title of his talk on 4/11 is

"Neutrino hunting with ANITA experiment"

Please mark your calendar and remember to join the event!
