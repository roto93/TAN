Dear all,

This week we have 2 colloquium talks, one on Wednesday (1/16) and the 
other on Thursday (1/17) (shifted from Friday due to Faculty Retreat).

present his talk titled

"Cosmological Physics with Black Holes",

Please note the special dates of the talks!  Mark your calendar and

and listen to the colloquium talks broadcast live from our website.

ARO: Call for Proposals (Spring 2008)
--------------------------------------

Dear all,

ASIAA's ARO proposal deadline for Spring 2008 (February - June)
is set on January 27, 2008 (Sunday).

Please refer to http://aro.as.arizona.edu/ for more information
on the telescopes and observations.  People are required to use
the KP12M and SMT proposal cover/summary sheets
( http://aro.as.arizona.edu/proposals and
  http://aro.as.arizona.edu/~aro/proposal_submission_information.htm )
for their proposals.

For electronic submission, ASIAA only accepts proposals in either
PS or PDF format.  Please submit your proposals by midnight
January 27, 2008 to kuan@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw ; no late proposals will
be accepted.


Kind regards,
Yi-Jehng Kuan
