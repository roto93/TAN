   Announcement of TIARA Winter School on Star Formation 
                           5 Feb - 10 Feb, 2009 
         National Tsing Hua University, Hsinchu, Taiwan 

The aim of this winter school is to provide an expository overview of 
the observational and theoretical understanding of star formation. The 
program includes molecular clouds, prestellar cores, low mass star 
formation, high mass star formation, jets, and outflows. Due to 
resource constraints, the participants are limited to 50 
graduate/advanced undergraduate students.
