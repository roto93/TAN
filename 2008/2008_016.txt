
Faculty Positions, National Cheng Kung University, Taiwan. 

Applicants are invited for faculty positions. Qualified candidates must have
a Ph.D. degree and demonstrate research capability in the following fields:
(1) plasma science (experiment and/or theory/simulation), and (2) space
science instrumentation. The appointment will begin on August 1, 2008.

research experience and plans, curriculum vitae, and three letters of
recommendation before March 31, 2008 to Prof. C. Z. (Frank) Cheng, Institute
of Space, Astrophysical and Plasma Science, National Cheng Kung University,
