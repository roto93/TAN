ANNOUNCEMENT OF TAIWAN TIARA WORKSHOP ON DIM KBOS
           (Hsinchu, Taiwan, DEC. 8-12, 2008)

Dear Colleagues,

To celebrate the initial publication of the first results from 
the TAOS project in search of distant, sub-km KBOs, a workshop
on dim KBOs will be held on the campus of National Tsing Hua 
University in Hsinchu this December.  The current list of 
international invited speakers includes

Charles Alcock (CfA)
Benjamin C. Bromley (U. Utah)
Mike Brown (Caltech)
Martin Duncan (Queens University)
Hal F. Levison (SWRI) (TBD)
Renu Malhotra (U. Arizona)
Alessandro Morbidelli (CNRS)
Steve S. Murray (CfA)
Re'em Sari (Hebrew University & Caltech)
Francoise Roques (Paris Observatory) (through video link, only)

The TAOS team and the NTHU team will also present their results.
Please visit the TIARA website for an up-to-date information.

Workshop website:
http://www.tiara.sinica.edu.tw/activities/workshop/20081208/
