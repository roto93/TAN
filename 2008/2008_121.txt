IAU individual membership 

Dear Colleagues,

as shown in the IAU web site
(http://iau.org/administration/membership/individual/qualification/), 
individuals intending to apply for IAU membership should submit applications
to their National Committee by Dec. 1, 2008. The National Committee should
submit their recommendation to IAU by March 1, 2009. 

I hereby, as the President of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of
China, invite you to send your application to me at <hkchang@phys.nthu.edu.tw>
by Dec. 1, 2008, if you are qualified and interested. Please visit the
aforementioned IAU web page for more details. The Executive Council of the
Society will discuss all the applications and make recommendation in
December 2008.
