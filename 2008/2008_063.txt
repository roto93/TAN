                CAST 2008 -- the Deadline is approaching!

Dear Colleagues,

   The 2008 Annual Meeting of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taiwan)
will be held at National Hualien University of Education on May 30 to
June 1, 2008.  The deadline for registration and submission is April 30.
Please visit the CAST 2008 website at 
http://www.cast.org.tw/cas97/index_e.php
(or, the CAST website at http://www.cast.org.tw/)   
for more information if you are planning to attend.  We are looking
forward to meet you with the beautiful scenery and relaxed atmosphere
in Hualien.
