          III "Guillermo Haro" Astrophysics Conference on 
          ------------------------------------------------

	        Cosmic Evolution and Galaxy Formation:
                 Structure, Interactions, and Feedback

                            hosted by the

         Instituto Nacional de Astrofisica, Optica y Electronica
        
                     Tonantzintla, Puebla, MEXICO.

                        November 22-26, 1999. 

Please circulate this message, and fill and return the electronic 
pre-registration form included below. 

Further information about the event will be sent to all those 
who do pre-register. 

All the information contained in this email, and future emails, 
will be available at our website http://www.inaoep.mx/~cosmo99/

This conference is part of the Programa de Investigacion en Astrofisica
Avanzada Guillermo Haro (http://www.inaoep.mx/~progharo/) created 
in 1995 by INAOE.

                        ********************
                        TOPICS TO BE COVERED
                        ********************

 I) Galaxy Formation: Cosmic Background and Lyman alpha forest. Dark Matter and
                 structure

 II) Galaxy Evolution: Clusters and individual galaxies (colors, evolution, 
                 X-rays, gas, B-fields, chemical abundances, masses, ages, 
                 interactions, star formation, and AGN)
 III) Star Formation and Evolution: Star formation in the Galaxy and external 
                 galaxies (IMF, masses, metallicities and primordial star 
                 formation). Spectrophotometric evolution

 IV) Feedback: Gas and H_2 formation. Energy injection from stars (HII regions, 
                 Supernovae, WR, PN, superbubbles). The role of dust.


 V) The Future: Perspectives for future research on star formation at different 
                 epochs with the new telescopes and computational capabilities.
