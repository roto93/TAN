Dear Colleague,

We believe that it is now the time to organise an `unspecialized' meeting
devoted to groups and clusters in relation with cosmology.


We therefore have the pleasure of announcing the XVIth IAP meeting


        CONSTRUCTING THE UNIVERSE WITH CLUSTERS OF GALAXIES


Planned on the week of July 3, 2000 at the Institut d'Astrophysique,
Paris France


The various topics to be covered are:

        Cluster formation
        Physics and observations of the intra-cluster medium:
                cooling flows, multiphase models, temperature gradients,
                cooling and heating processes, magnetic field,
                cosmic rays
        Dark matter: simulations and observations (X-rays,
                     Sunyaev-Zel'dovich effect)
        Galaxies in clusters: luminosity functions, segregation,
                differences with field galaxies
        The future: instruments, numerical simulations,
        et coetera.


The Scientific Organizing Committee members are:

M. Arnaud, R. de Carvalho, G. Evrard, A. Fabian, W. Forman, M. Geller, 
I. Gioia, N. Kaiser, Y. Rephaeli, E. Salvador Sole, J. Silk, Y.Tanaka, S.White


The Technical Organizing Committee members are :

B. Raban (secretariat), X. Nathan, L. Provost, N. Royer, F. Warin


Information will be available on http://www.iap.fr/Conferences


Looking forward to seeing you in Paris next year

Daniel Gerbal (organizer)
Florence Durret (co-organizer)
