                          POST DOCTORAL FELLOWSHIP

                                     at

                          ONSALA SPACE OBSERVATORY

              The Swedish National Facility for Radio Astronomy



We are pleased to announce the availability of a Postdoctoral Fellowship at
Onsala Space Observatory. The 2-year position, funded by the Swedish
Foundation for International Co-operation in Research and Higher Education,
is available now to young, non-Swedish scientists who received their
doctorate less than three years ago.

The research at Onsala covers most areas of radio astronomy with special
reference to millimetre techniques and VLBI. We welcome applications from
candidates with interests (observational or theoretical) in the Interstellar
Medium, Cosmic Masers, Molecular properties of Galaxies, nearby or at high
red-shift, Quasars or Active Galactic Nuclei.

Interested candidates should contact Professor Roy Booth, Director of the
observatory, enclosing a Curriculum Vitae and a list of publications, as
soon as possible.  Information about the Observatory is available at our
website:    http://www.oso.chalmers.se/ .
