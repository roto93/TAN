Subject: Version 5.0 of "Guidelines for VLBA Observations"


Version 5.0 of "Guidelines for VLBA Observations" is now available on the
NRAO WWW site (via links VLBA -> Observing with the VLBA).  The principal
substantive changes are: (1) a partial relaxation of some mode-switching
restrictions; and (2) the incorporation of several general limitations on
the use of Mark 4 terminals in observations to be processed at the VLBA
correlator.  The Mark 4 material has been moved into the Guidelines from
the "OK Modes" document, which has become obsolete now that all individual
modes have been validated.
