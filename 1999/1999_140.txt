Dear Colleagues and friends,

We were all very moved by the messages and expressions of sympathy 
we received from you and from many other colleagues during the
days which followed the terrible accident of the telepherique at the Plateau de Bure
which took the lives of 20 people. We thank you very much for this
expression of solidarity. 

Many of you offered to help. We would like to inform you and through
you as many other colleagues as possible that the "Fondation de France
has opened a solidarity account to help the families of the victims. 
A special committee, chaired by Dr. Catherine Brechignac, Director General of CNRS, 
will make sure that the money reaches the families as soon as possible and 
according to their individual needs.

If you want to contribute to the support of these families, you can
send a check or money order made out to the :
