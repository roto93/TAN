> The EVN VLBI School will be held at the Joint Institute for VLBI in
> Europe (JIVE), Dwingeloo, The Netherlands from Wednesday 03 through
> Friday 05 November 1999. The three day School will consist of a series
> of lectures, interleaved with practical demonstrations, that cover all
> aspects of VLBI - from observation planning to post-correlation data
> reduction.
> 
> VLBI technique offers an unrivaled angular resolution on the order of
> milli- and sub-milliarcseconds in studying celestial radio sources.
> Researchers with no or limited experience in VLBI technique are
> encouraged to attend the School. Full details of the proposed program
> will be given in the Second Announcement and placed on the EVN VLBI
> School WWW site
>
