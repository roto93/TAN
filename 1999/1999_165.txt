interstellar chemistry. I will review the history of our knowledge of H3+,
the discovery of it in molecular clouds, recent follow-up observations
which have led to its discovery in the diffuse interstellar medium, and
the chemistry and astrophysics which affect its observed abundances in
these two environments.
