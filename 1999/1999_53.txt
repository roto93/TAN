AAO/ATNF POST DOCTORAL RESEARCH FELLOWSHIP

Applications are invited for an Anglo-Australian Observatory/Australia
Telescope National Facility (AAO/ATNF)post-doctoral research Fellowship.
The AAO/ATNF Fellowship will be tenable for up to two years from July,
1999. The position will be based at the laboratories of the institutions in
Epping, near Sydney, NSW. 

The recipient of the fellowship will be free to conduct their own research
program, and the appointee will be expected to participate in the operation
of the Observatories by performing some duties such as supporting visiting
astronomers for about a quarter of their time. The Fellowship salary will
be in the range $A42,504 to $A43,562 p.a., depending on research
experience. A relocation allowance of $A6,000 is also payable, in addition
to airfare to Australia (and return on completion of the appointment).
Closing date May 3, 1999.

Further information about the Fellowship and the full text of the
advertisement can be obtained from
