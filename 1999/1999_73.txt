                   SECOND ANNOUNCEMENT


             "ISO Surveys of a Dusty Universe"

     Workshop at Ringberg Castle, November 8 - 12, 1999


A one week workshop will be held at Ringberg Castle of the
Max-Planck-Society about 50 km south of Munich in the Bavarian Alps
from 08 to 12 Nov 1999.  Topics to be covered include the ISO deep
surveys, extragalactic background radiation, surveys dedicated to
selected groups of galaxies (field and cluster galaxies, ULIRGs,
quasars, etc.), and complementary surveys.  These sessions will be
accompanied by contributions on theoretical work on galaxy
formation. One session will be devoted to galactic topics such as
pre-protostellar clouds and young stellar objects with disks.

The workshop starts on Monday at 14.00h.  Arrival at the castle is
either Sunday evening (16.00h .. 22.00h) or Monday morning.  Wednesday
afternoon is partially free for informal get-togethers; because of the
remote location of the castle there is no quick access to Munich. In
the late afternoon there will be a poster session (with coffee) until
dinner at 18.30h. A guided tour through the castle and its history,
architecture, paintings, furniture, etc. will be offered at one of the
evenings. The workshop officially ends on Friday with lunch at 14.00h.

After the first announcement, a large number of very interesting
contributions were received.  Several talks, mostly major contributions
based on ISO Survey results and a few complementary topics, including
some of the speakers, have already been preselected by the SOC to
structure the meeting. This preliminary agenda serves as a skeleton of
the workshop and covers roughly 50 % of the total of about 60 oral
contributions planned.  The length of the talks will be typically 20
min, and a number of posters will also be accepted.  Selection of most
contributions will take place in June 1999, and very few slots for
important last minute results will be filled in in September 1999.

There will be a moderate conference fee to cover the proceedings,
coffee, etc., the exact amount of which will be determined after the
financial support of the workshop has been settled.

The deadline for registration including abstract will be June 1, 1999.
Please use the attached Registration Form, or the LaTeX template, to
be found on the workshop homepage http://isowww.mpia-hd.mpg.de/iso_surveys/,
and send it to ISO.SURVEYS@mpia-hd.mpg.de.  The final agenda will be
distributed with the Third Announcement in late summer.
