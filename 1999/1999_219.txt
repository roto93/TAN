            TITLE:   Reconstruction of the initial density field 
                     through galaxy spins 
 
                     
                            ABSTRACT 

   In collaboration with Professor Ue-Li Pen, I have developed a new 
   technique to reconstruct the initial density field of the universe 
   by means of measuring galaxy spins. 
   Traditionally, the peculiar velocity field was used to construct the 
   density field. But the peculiar velocitis are much harder to measure 
   observationally than the galaxy spins.
   I will introduce the linear theory for the angular momentum 
   of protogalaxies, and will explain how the prediction of the linear 
   theory can be used to reconstruct the initial density field from 
   the observed galaxy angular momenta.
