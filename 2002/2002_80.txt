                 Title:  Gravitational Lensing in Clusters of Galaxies

                 Abstract:

                 Clusters of galaxies acting as gravitational lenses 
                 distort the shapes of background galaxy images
                 by virtue of their large mass scales and compactness.
                 The observable image distortions in turn enable us to
                 investigate directly the mass distribution including 
                 invisible dark matter. The talk will be on gravitational
                 lensing phenomena in clusters, and be mainly on weak 
                 lensing.
