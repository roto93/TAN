Dear All,

Pulsars, the neutron stars that send powerful
"lighthouse beams" through the Universe, have
been observed to have faster proper motion than
expected, resulting in the conjecture that neutron
stars are "kicked" at birth. Prof. Qiu-He Peng of
Nanjing University will talk about the origin of this
kick that causes the furious acceleration of neutron
stars. Please join us next Monday.

******* ASIAA Luncheon Talk/Journal Club ******************
Time      : 12pm, monday  July 8th, 2002.
Place     : our conference room 716.
Speaker   : Prof. Peng, Qiu-He (Nanjing University)
Topic     : The Origin of Kick Velocity of Pulsars

Note    : Please send the request for lunch box to
          me (gu@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw) by 10:30 am,
          July 8th. As usual, no request no lunch box.
