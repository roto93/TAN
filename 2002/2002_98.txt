5) LOCAL INFORMATION
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Taipei, the capital of Republic of China, is located at north of 
Taiwan. National Taiwan University, located at southwest of Taipei
City, is surrounded by various shops, eateries, and theaters. The
subway system with a station just outside the campus builds the 
convenient connections to main features of the city and to the 
scenic area along the north shore of Taiwan. 

Weather of north Taiwan in late May and early June is a little hot, 
with temperatures 22-30 C. Rain is usual, 50% chance of raining and 
80% of relative humidity. 

Electric outlet in Taiwan is 110 V double straight poles.
