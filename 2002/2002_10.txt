Dear Reader,
  The Smithsonian Astrophysical Observatory (SAO) and the Institute of Astronomy 
& Astrophysics (ASIAA) of the Academia Sinica in Taiwan today held a joint press 
conference at the 199th Meeting of the Americal Astronomical Society (AAS) in 
Washington, DC., to report progress in commissioning the Sub-Millimeter Array 
(SMA), as well as the first scientific images from this telescope.  The SMA 
comprises eight 6-m antennas located on Mauna Kea, two of which are built in 
Taiwan.  It is the first interferometric array to make images at sub-millimeter 
wavelengths.
  In conjunction with this event, the ASIAA is issuing a press release in Taiwan 
in parallel with that made earlier today (Wednesday) in the US.  This press 
release can be found on our WEB page:
  
  http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/SMAPR/
