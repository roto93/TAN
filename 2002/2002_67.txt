Workshop on Galactic Dynamics

JENAM 2002, Porto, Portugal September 2--7, 2002


We are happy to invite you to take part in a workshop on galaxies and
galactic dynamics at the JENAM meeting in Porto. This invitation is open
to all registered participants at JENAM 2002. The costs of the
participation in the workshop are included in the JENAM registration fee.

Scientific Topics:

The scientific programme of the workshop will be made up from
contributions by participants; an update of workshop participants and
titles/abstracts of presentations will be available here.

The topics we propose to cover at this workshop are:

    * The Milky Way.
    * Galactic nuclei w/without black holes.
    * Galactic dynamics, including galactic satellites (dwarfs, GC's
systems).
    * Stellar dynamics: stellar clusters, open and globular.

Structure of the Workshop:

Crucial to a successful workshop is the need to balance out theoretical
and observational work. How this is achieved in practice depends on the
response from participants at the meeting. We will allow for review
speakers covering the four major themes of the workshop; these 40-minute
reviews can then be followed by 20 minute talks. Time will also be
allocated (10 minutes) for poster presentations or, if pressed for time, a
session chair will be asked to summarise a set of posters.

In as much as this is possible we will seek to attribute space to young
and experienced researchers randomly in the programme, to help
interactions.

In this way we can accommodate from 8 to 9 speakers per session, times
four half-day session. We are leaving out one afternoon as safety against
time-compression. The (roughly) 36 presentations scheduled would match, or
approach, what was done at previous JENAM meetings.

We are also looking into the possibility of short demonstrations of data
reduction packages or simulation software, which could be compressed in
short presentations to a large audience, or as an open,
individual-oriented format in a room during a specified afternoon. We have
specifically laptop-type presentations in mind. If you would be interested
in such an exercise, please let us know directly.


Registration deadlines :

In order to coordinate the programme with the JENAM schedule please check
all the deadlines for registration set by the LOC. Only registered JENAM
participants can attend workshops but of course our doors will remain open
to all of them wishing to contribute.

In addition, we would appreciate if you could also write to the conveners
(email: jenam2002@ari.uni-heidelberg.de, with subject ``JENAM 2002'')
directly, stating your name, affiliation and the title of your
presentation. It is not necessary to write an abstract of your work. If
you wish to take part in an open session on computer software, please
mention this briefly in your message: for instance, would you present a
package yourself or simply attend? This will help us get a feel for
how many potential participants would take part in such a session.

More details of format and logistics will be provided at the meeting.

We hope to hear from you in Porto!
