First Circular

2003 Symposium of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taipei)

New Physics Building, National Taiwan University, Taipei
April 18-19, 2003

As part of the 2003 Assembly of the Chinese Astronomical Society (Taipei),
this symposium will serve as an annual meeting for astronomers and
astrophysicists in Taiwan to exchange their ideas, to present their recent
research, and to promote their collaborations.
The Program Committee sincerely invite you to participate. 
We call for oral and poster papers in all fields of astronomy.
Other activities of the Assembly will be held on April 19, 2003.
