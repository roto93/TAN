            Colloquium (2:00 pm)
                                                                       
            SPEAKER : Dr. Huan Lin
                      (Steward Observatory)   

            Title: Evolution of Galaxy Populations
                          in the CNOC2 Redshift Survey


            Abstract:

   I will present results on the evolution of different galaxy
   populations at intermediate redshifts 0.1 < z < 0.6, 
   as determined from a large sample of 5000 galaxies taken from 
   the CNOC2 Field Galaxy Redshift Survey.

   I will describe the CNOC2 data set, as well as the methods used
   to classify different CNOC2 populations from UBVRI photometry.
   Results will be presented on the redshift evolution of the
   luminosity functions of early-, intermediate-, and late-type 
   CNOC2 galaxy populations, and the observations will be
   cast in the context of simple galaxy evolution models.

   Finally, some preliminary results will be shown on the evolution
   of the star formation rate and stellar mass functions of
   CNOC2 galaxies.
