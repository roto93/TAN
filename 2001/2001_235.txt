Title: Possible evidence for a variable fine structure constant
       from QSO absorption lines
Speaker: Ma Cheng-Jiun
Time: 12pm, Monday, Nov. 19, 2001
Location: Room 716, the Condensed Matter and Physics Dept Building
          of the National Taiwan University


IS THE FINE STRUCTURE CONSTANT CHANGING? This idea was
hypothesized by Paul Dirac in 1937. Ma Cheng-Jiun will
answer for us based upon the study of sampling the anceint
light emitted from distant quasers but absorbed by ancient
atoms in less distant gas clouds lying in front of quasars.
