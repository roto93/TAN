             First International ASTROD School and Symposium
          on Laser Astrodynamics, Space Test of Relativity and
                      Gravitational-Wave Astronomy

            National Astronomical Observatories, Beijing, China
                        September 13-23, 2001

                          FIRST ANNOUNCEMENT

Sponsored by: National Astronomical Observatories CAS (NAOCAS, Beijing);  
              Center for Gravitation and Cosmology (CGC, Hsinchu).

This conference consists of two parts. The first part is in the form of 
school (Sept. 13-19) mainly for young people who are becoming interested 
in these topics. The second part is the symposium (Sept. 20-23). Language 
for the School and Symposium is English.

Principal Topics:

  -  Astrodynamics, ephemeris, and mission orbit design/simulation;
  -  Solar seismology and solar models;
  -  Tests and empirical foundations of relativistic gravity;
  -  Detection of gravitational waves and gravitational-wave astronomy;
  -  Technological development for space laser ranging;
  -  ASTROD mission concept study.
