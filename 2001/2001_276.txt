                 Title:  Cometary Ejection and Meteoroid Stream

                 Abstract:

                 A Comet ejects dust particles when moving around
                 perihelion to form meteoroid stream along its orbit. 
                 The orbital plane and the period of the meteoroid 
                 are slightly different from those of the parent comet 
                 because of ejection, solar radiation pressure, planetary
                 perturbation, and so on. The talk is about these aspects, 
                 especially the initial ejection velocity.
