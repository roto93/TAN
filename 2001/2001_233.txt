Dear Collegue,

The next Rencontres de Moriond workshop will be held on
march 16-23 2002 and will be devoted to :

   The Cosmological Model

The spirit will be to step back and look at the journey from the
beginning of modern cosmology until today, and beyond.
The program is presently being worked out and includes:
- primordial nucleosynthesis
- CMB physics
- structure formation
- weak lensing
- galaxy clusters
- galaxy surveys
- nature of dark matter
- physics of the early Universe

It will appear on the web site of the conference
( http://moriond.in2p3.fr/J02/ )
and regularly updated there.

You can find registration forms and other organizational details
on this web site.
