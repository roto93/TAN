Dear Past OVRO proposers,

The deadline for the fall OVRO Millimeter Array proposals, Sept. 5th, is fast 
approaching.  This year, we are  encouraging internal and external proposers 

a) to prepare OVRO proposals using a standard latex and style file 
   generated by Kartik Sheth
b) to email one gzipped postscript copy of the proposal AFTER 
   NAMING IT APPROPRIATELY  to ovroprop@astro.caltech.edu.
   Naming conventions and detailed instructions may be found at 
	http://www.ovro.caltech.edu/mm/proposals/prop_prop.html

This site has links to the new style file as well.

The account  ovroprop@astro.caltech.edu forwards proposals to Shardha Jogee
and Ralf Launhardt for processing.  The email submission provides a 
time-stamped postscript copy of proposals on disk and this improves 
book-keeping, makes it easy to track missing pages, and enables color 
figures to be emailed  to main reviewers and so forth. Thus, even 
internal people are encouraged to submit a copy by email. (You can 
deliver a hard copy in addition to the emailed versions if you so 
wish).

   Best Regards,

   Shardha
