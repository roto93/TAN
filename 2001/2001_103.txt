               Hsinchu Astrophysics Summer School 2001
                             (HASS 2001)

                     " The Cosmic Expansion"

                 Hsinchu, Taiwan, August 28-30, 2001

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

Aiming at providing students and junior researchers a compact, 
introductory, and up-to-date course on different aspects of the cosmic
expansion and cosmology, the National Center for Theoretical Sciences
(NCTS) is organizing a school entitled "Hsinchu Astrophysics 
Summer School 2001 (HASS 2001) on the Cosmic Expansion".

Lecturers of this school include:
Dr. Peter Dunsby (University of Cape Town, South Africa)
-- Einstein's Field Equations and Cosmological Models
Prof. Howard Yee (University of Toronto, Canada)
-- Galaxy Redshift Surveys and Cosmology
Dr. Peter Hoeflich (University of Texas at Austin, USA)
-- Supernovae as Distance Indicators

This school will be held at the campus of National Tsing Hua University 
in Hsinchu, Taiwan, on August 28-30, 2001. You are cordially invited to
attend. There is no registration fee requirement. Lecture notes and lunch 
will be provided without expense to registered participants. 
Free local accommodation can be arranged upon request.
