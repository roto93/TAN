This meeting is aimed at assembling astronomers in the Asian-Pacific
region.  The goal of the meeting is (1) to exchange the results of
recent research, (2) to promote research exchanges and collaborations
in the region, (3) to encourage young astronomers' activity and to
nurture next generation researchers, and (4) to discuss the way of
driving research activities in the region and popularizing astronomy.

For further information please visit the web-site

    http://www.astro.isas.ac.jp/conference/aprm2002/
