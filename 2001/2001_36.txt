First Circular

                  The 8th Taipei Astrophysics Workshop
   "AMiBA 2001: High-z Clusters, Missing Baryons, and CMB Polarization"

                Taipei & Hualien, Taiwan 11-15 June 2001
                   http://www.asiaa.sinica.edu.tw/taw8


The National Taiwan University (Physics Department) and Academia Sinica
(Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics) are jointly organizing The
Eighth Taipei Astrophysics Workshop  (TAW8) entitled "AMiBA 2001: High-z
Clusters, Missing Baryons, and CMB Polarization", from Monday 11 June to
Friday 15 June inclusive.  The workshop will be held at the National
Taiwan University in Taipei for the first 2 days, and the following 3 days
will be at a resort hotel in the Taroko National Park on the East Coast of
Taiwan.

(The train trips between Taipei and Hualien along the East Coast of Taiwan 
will be arranged by the organizers.  The travel expenses are included in the
registration fee.)

If you wish to attend the meeting, please  register electronically: complete 
and return the appended registration form (also available from the TAW8 
homepage) to taw8@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw.  TAW8 invited speakers please also 
register electronically.

The deadline for registration is 30/Apr/2001.

Programmes and Speakers
========================
The workshop is aimed at the main science topics relevant to the
AMiBA (Array for Microwave Background Anisotropy) project:

         --CMB 
         --Clusters of galaxies 
         --ICM, Lensing, and baryons in galaxy clusters 
         --IGM, Missing baryons in large scale structure

Format of Workshop
==================
The workshop contains a series of invited talks, including 1 hr review and
1/2 hr current results talks.  In addition, there will be a poster session
and some 15-min contributed talks.  


Contributed talks and posters
==============================
Please submit the abstract of your contribution to
taw8@asiaa.sinica.edu.tw no later than 31/Mar/2001.


Workshop Proceedings
====================
The workshop proceedings will be published by the Astronomical Society of 
the Pacific as part of the ASP Conference Series.  Registered participants
will receive a copy.


Registration Fee
================
The registration fee is NT$7500 (approx. 250 US Dollars), it is reduced to 
NT$2200  (approx. 75 US Dollars) for students.  It should be paid in New 
Taiwan Dollars at the registration desk of the workshop.   

The registration fee will cover the costs of the Taipei/Hualien train
trips, workshop proceedings, and workshop banquet.
