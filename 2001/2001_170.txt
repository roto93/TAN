> Introduction
> ------------
> The 4th Asian Workshop on First-Principles Electronic Structure
> Calculations will be held in Taiwan at International Conference Hall,
> Condensed Matter & Physics Building, National Taiwan University, Taipei on
> 29-31 October 2001.
>
> This workshop series is devoted to recent advances in the first-principles
> electronic structure calculations mainly in condensed matter physics.
> Activities in this field have been promoted recently in Asian countries
> and collaborations and exchange of researchers have been gradually
> growing. The main purpose of this workshop series is to promote
> communication among Asian countries and to advance the research activities
> in the electronic structure calculations.
>
> The coming workshop follows the first one held in Tsukuba (1998), second
> one in Seoul (1999) and third one in Tsukuba again (2000). Besides
> participants from Taiwan, Japan, Korea and other Asian countries, several
> leading researchers from USA and Europe will be invited.
>
