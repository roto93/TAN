         SPEAKER: Andrew Liddle (University of Sussex)

         TITLE: "The Origin and Evolution of Structures in the Universe"


ABSTRACT

	The key to understanding the global properties of the 
	Universe lies in understanding how structures within 
	it, such as galaxies and irregularities in the cosmic 
	microwave background, evolve. This talk will overview 
	the exciting observational developments currently driving 
	cosmology, describing the best current ideas as to how 
	the Universe works and describing possible implications 
	for the physics of the very early Universe.
