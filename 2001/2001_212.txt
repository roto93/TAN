> Astroparticle physics is a new frontier between astrophysics and particle
> physics, and is a rapidly developing field of research. Gamma rays,
> neutrinos and cosmic rays are presently the main source of information
> about the highest energy phenomenon occurring in the cosmos. The particle
> physics aspects of dark matter present in the cosmos is yet another
> interesting topic in this regard.
>
> In this "First NCTS Workshop on Astroparticle Physics", the review
> lectures will cover the above-mentioned topics, whereas the shorter talks
> will focus on the more recent progress in the same area. The review
> lectures will be delivered by F. Halzen (Wisconsin) and K. Olive
> (Minnesota), while the shorter talks are currently arranged by
> invitations.
>
