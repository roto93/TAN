            Colloquium (2:00 pm)

            SPEAKER : Prof. Chen Yuan Dong
                      (Dept. of Physics, NTU)
                      
            Title: Multiphoton Fluoresence Microscopy - 
                     Bioimaging Deeper and Better
                     
            Abstract:  Since its introduction in 1990, multiphoton fluorescence 
microscopy has developed into a major branch of modern optical microscopy. Based 
on non-linear molecular excitation, multiphoton microscopy induces simultaneous 
absorption of two or more near-infrared photons from fluorescent molecules. The 
subsequent fluorescence emission from these molecules may be detected for 
bioimaging applications. In this presentation, I will discuss the principles and 
advantages of this technique along with applications in autofluorescent skin 
imaging, transdermal drug delivery, and tissue engineering. I will conclude with 
a description of future technical development of this technology in National 
Taiwan University.
