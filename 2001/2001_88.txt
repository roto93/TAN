Wells, to be held in Asilomar, California (near Monterey) Sept. 10-14 2001.

http://www.iquest.ucsb.edu/ITQW'01.htm. This list gives one an idea of the
remarkable breadth and depth of this field. More invited speakers will be
added, including several selected from among the contributed abstracts.  I
am looking forward to a very exciting conference

ABSTRACT SUBMISSION:  May 1, 2001
NOTIFICATION OF ACCEPTANCE:  Before May 31, 2001
EARLY CONFERENCE REGISTRATION:  July 15, 2001

be found on the updated web site.  Please also remember that we are limiting

the web site.
