Dear colleague,

the astrophysical institutions of Munich (Max-Planck-Institutes, University
Observatory, and ESO) will host this year's JENAM meeting (Joint European
and National Astronomy Meeting, organised by European Astronomical
Society and the Astronomische Gesellschaft) Sep. 10-15, 2001.

Besides a few review talks of general nature there will be mainly so-called
mini-symposia focusing on specific subjects. I would like to draw your
attention to a mini-symposium organized by the undersigned conveners
on "Evolution of Galaxies - Observation and Dynamics" (Dynamics,
Evolution, and low surface brightness galaxies). 
There will be both talks and poster contributions. More info can be found at
our WWW page at 

http://www.astro.ruhr-uni-bochum.de/haberzet/JENAM01/ms5.html 

and at the JENAM-2001 WWW page at

http://www.mpa-garching.mpg.de/english/conferences/jenam01/

It is hoped that the mini-symposium, consisting of three sessions of 
90 minutes plus one hour poster session attracts your interest and
kindly invite you to come to this meeting. 
Abstracts for talks and/or posters are due until July 13th and
should be sent both to 

jenam-ms5@ari.uni-heidelberg.de        (MS5 conveners)  and
schie@astro.uni-jena.de                (secretary Astronomische Gesellschaft)

We reserve the right to accept only a limited number of talks, subject to
availability of time. The selection process will be supervised by the SOC
whose members are published on our webpage. In accord with the general
policy of JENAM meetings, however, all efforts will be done to accept
as many poster contributions as possible.                

To submit your abstract please use the abstract Latex macro available at:

http://www.astro.uni-jena.de/Astron_Ges/jeabsmac.tex

Please be aware that sending an abstract via the above procedure DOES NOT
SUBSTITUTE A PROPER REGISTRATION WITH THE MAIN CONFERENCE organisation,
possible via the above web-links.

With best wishes,

the conveners of MS5
