> ----------------------------------------------------------------
>   This meeting is aimed at assembling astronomers in the Asian-Pacific
> region.  The goal of the meeting is (1) to exchange the results of
> recent research, (2) to promote research exchanges and collaborations
> in the region, (3) to encourage young astronomers' activity and to
> nurture next generation researchers, and (4) to discuss the way of
> driving research activities in the region and popularizing astronomy.
>   In this second circular, we call for papers (oral/poster) and
> registration for the meeting. We hope to have responses from many
> active researchers in this region.
>

> Registration:
>      Those who intend to attend the meeting should register via the
>      conference web-site.
>      The registration fee is 10,000 yen by March 31, and 14,000 yen after
>      March 31.  (1 us-dollar is approx. 124 yen on Nov. 26)
>      Each accompanied person is charged 6000 yen.   The conference
>      banquet is included.
>      All participants from Japan need to pay the fee to the conference
>      postal saving account (Yu-bin chokin Ko-za) to complete the
>      registration.
>      Participants from abroad should pay the fee at the registration
>      desk by cash (Japanese yen only).
>      They are still required to register no later than
>      March 31 to appreciate the early registration discount.
>      Credit cards are not accepted.
>
> Abstract for Oral/Poster Papers:
>      Abstracts for oral or poster papers should be separately posted
>      on the web-site.  Participants will receive the abstract book
>      at the registration desk.
>
> One Paper Policy:
>      Because of the limited space of the conference venue, we need to
>      limit the numbers of papers to one presentation (either oral or
>      poster) for a registered participant.  Each participant can send
>      only one abstract.  One paper policy does not apply to the
>      invited talk.
>
> Proceedings:
>      Proceedings of the meeting will be printed in two volumes.
>      The first volume (Vol.I) will be published by Astronomical Society
>      of the Pacific as the APS conference series. This volume will
>      contain all the oral papers.  We will offer a discount price (under
$60)
>      for the participants who order the proceedings at the
>      registration desk during the meeting.
>      The second volume (Vol.II), whose charge is included in the
>      registration fee, will be separately published by the LOC.  All
>      the poster papers will be in this volume.
>      Both Vol.I and II will be registered on ADS.
>
> Support:
>      Limited amount of travel support for young astronomers (those who
>      were born in/after 1962) may be available.  One can apply for
>      this support via the conference web-site when she/he
>      registers but no later than March 31.
>
> Accommodation:
>      Information of hotels with discount rates supplied by a travel
>      agency (Kinki Nippon Tourist) can be found on the conference
>      web-site.  Please download the application form and send it back
>      directly to the travel agency by fax.
>
> Satellite Meetings:
>      Several satellite meetings are planned right after the meeting
>      (July 5 and 6).  These meetings are independent of the main
>      meeting. Registrations and abstracts for the papers should be
>      separately posted to the organizers of the satellite meetings.
>      One paper policy of the main meeting does not apply to the
>      satellite meetings.
