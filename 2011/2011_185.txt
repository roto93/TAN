The Department of Physics of Osaka University is calling for
applications to its International Physics Course (IPC).

It offers Master and Ph.D. courses on a wide range of research topics
in Physics (see also the webpage below for more details).

Most successful applicants will receive a scholarship.

Education and research is conducted in English (but students are
welcome to learn a little of Japanese language and culture).

Applications for the admission to academic year 2012/13 (starting from
October 1, 2012) are open from from November 1 to December 10, 2011.

Website: http://www.rcnp.osaka-u.ac.jp/~IPC/
