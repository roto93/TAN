First Announcement (February 7, 2011)

4th East Asia VLBI Workshop --
Sciences and Technology by now and future EAVN

18 - 20 April 2011, Kunming, China

Yunnan Astronomical Observatory, Shanghai Astronomical Observatory, NAOC,
CAS Sponsored jointly by: CAS Key Lab for Radio Astronomy


Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of the East Asia VLBI Consortium, we are glad to announce that the
4th East Asia VLBI workshop will take place during 18-20 April 2011 in Kunming,
Yunnan of China.

The first phase of East Asia VLBI Network (EAVN) observations started in 2010.
The developments of telescopes, instruments and VLBI correlator (KJJVC) are
making steady progress. It is expected that the EAVN will be able to achieve
some more remarkable sciences. As a continuous effort in promoting the VLBI
collaboration, the main motivation of the coming workshop (4th of its series)
is to review the newest developments in both instrumentation and science, and
to discuss the future collaboration in various VLBI-related fields.

You are cordially invited. Please express/confirm your participation by filling
registration form attached (omitted by TAN). Comments and questions can be
addressed to Dr. Min Wang (wm@ynao.ac.cn) or Dr. Kazuya Hachisuka
(khachi@shao.ac.cn) or Dr. Zhiqiang Shen (zshen@shao.ac.cn).

More details of the workshop will be provided in the 2nd announcement.

We are looking forward to seeing you in Kunming.


Best regards,
Min Wang, Kazuya Hachisuka and Zhiqiang Shen
On behalf of
East Asia VLBI Consortium
