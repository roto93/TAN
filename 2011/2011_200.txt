Dear all, below is the information for upcoming colloquium at IANCU;

Title: Multiwavelength studies of clusters associated with H II regions
Speaker: Dr. Anil Pandey (ARIES, Manora Peak, Nainital, India)
Date: 2011-12-09
Time: 14:00-15:00
Location: NCU  S4-1013
Abstract:

Since last few years we are pursuing multiwavelength studies of star-forming regions to
understand the star formation scenario and the initial mass function (IMF) in these
regions. The pre-main-sequence objects associated with the regions are identified on the
basis of near/ mid infrared colour excess, H_alpha emission and X-ray emission. The ages
of the young stellar sources have been estimated using the optical colour-magnitude
diagram, which indicate a non-coeval star formation in the clusters. On the basis of
recent observations we have found evidence for triggered star formation at the periphery
of cluster regions.

Link: http://www.astro.ncu.edu.tw/info/seminar/collo/201112091400.html
