Subject: Recent Updates in the ALMA Science Portal

Dear Potential ALMA Proposers,

   Science Portal (SP) site has been updated last Tuesday. Here is a summary
of major updates in SP forwarded by Okumura-san of EA-ARC:

- Capabilities: We have added a link to the antenna configuration files,
   text regarding this. In addition, we have added text regarding the
   preliminary antenna reconfiguration time schedule. The Proposers
   Guide (both html text and pdf doc) has been updated accordingly.
   (see http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/call-for-proposals/capabilities)

  - ALMA Technical Hand book has newly been released. OT Ref Manual
   and ALMA Sensitivity Calculator Guide have been updated. EA Users
   Guide has also been updated.
   (see http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/document-and-tools)

  - A countdown clock has been added to the SP
    (see the right side of http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/)

  - A weather page with statistics and an atmosphere model tool has been
    added to 'About ALMA'
    (http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/about-alma/weather/overview)

  - The statistics on "Notice of Intent" has been added. (see
    http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/call-for-proposals/notice-of-intent)

  - Science Verification page.
    (http://almascience.nao.ac.jp/alma-data/science-verification)
    User can propose to observe the source that is already or will be used
    for the Science Verification.

Best Regards,
Chin-Fei
On behalf of Taiwan ARC Node
