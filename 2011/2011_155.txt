Harvard

Topic: Math, Physics, and Calabi–Yau Manifolds
Time: 10/04 (Tuesday) 14:20 - 15:10
Place: Room 104, CCM-New Physics Building

Abstract: I’d like to talk about how mathematics and physics can come together
to the benefit of both fields, particularly in the case of Calabi-Yau spaces and
string theory. This happens to be the subject of the new book I coauthored, THE
SHAPE OF INNER SPACE, It also tells some of my own story and a bit of the
history of geometry as well.

猜想等重要數學定理。他囊括數學界所有的最高榮譽，包括Fields Medal、Crafoord Prize
以及 Wolf
