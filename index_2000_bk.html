Dear Astronomers and Astrophysicists,

An email network (TAN: Taiwan Astronomy Network) has been set up for all professional astronomers/astrophysicists in Taiwan or related to Taiwan. The aim of this network is to increase the communication among us in an INFORMAL way. TAN acts just like a real-time open forum and your messages will be distributed to all subscribers automatically. A good example of the usuage of TAN is information exchange. As mentioned, this is not an official network, so feel free to bring up anything you think is relevant to astronomy in Taiwan.

If you are interested in subscribing it, please email kuan@sinica.edu.tw for details. Please give a short description of your professional background. Graduate students in astronomy/astrophysics/physics programs are also welcome.

Comment? Suggestion? PLEASE PASS THE WORDS OF THE EXISTENCE OF TAN TO PEOPLE YOU THINK WHO SHOULD BE INCLUDED. To keep TAN alive, active participation from all subscribers are needed. Please GIVE IT A TRY! Hope we can hear from you very soon.
