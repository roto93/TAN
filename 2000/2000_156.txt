Dear Colleagues, 

************************REMINDER NOTICE******************************

**********AUSTRALIA TELESCOPE NATIONAL FACILITY ***********

Applications for observing time are now invited for the 2000OCT term 
for the following telescopes:

Compact Array, Narrabri 
Parkes telescope, Parkes 
Mopra telescope, Coonabarabran 
VLBI observations 

*********************************************************************** 
CLOSING DATE for applications is 12th July 2000 
For 3 month Observing term October 1st to December 30th 2000 
************************************************************************

For the Compact Array, proposers should note that the term will start in 
the 6A configuration. The other configurations offered for the term are the 
6C, 1.5B, 1.5C, 750B and 750C arrays.

In addition there may be a limited amount of testing/science time
available for 22 GHz observations using two of the Compact Array telescopes. 
22 GHz telescope applications should include an ATNF staff member. 
For further information on the 22 GHz system, please email Dave McConnell 
(dmcconne@atnf.csiro.au).

Application forms, telescope documentation and full information on how to 
apply for ATNF telescope time are available on the web at the address 
http://www.atnf.csiro.au/observers/

Please note that the scientific case for telescope applications should not 
exceed a total of 3 pages (including figures). A font size of at least 10 point

should be used.
