The 8th Asia Pacific Physics Conference, APPC 2000
Aug. 7 -- 10, 2000
Third Circular
http://www.phys.sinica.edu.tw/~appc2k
You are cordially invited to participate in APPC 2000, the 8th Asia
Pacific Physics Conference, to be held in the Institute of Physics,
Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan, on August 7 -- 10, 2000.  The
conference will include plenary, parallel, as well as poster sessions.
The official language is English.

TOPICS for APPC 2000
1. Applied Physics
2. Astrophysics and Cosmic Physics
3. Atomic, Molecular, Optical Physics, and Plasma Physics
4. Computational & Statistical Physics
5. Condensed Matter Physics
6. Nuclear Physics
7. Particles and Fields
8. Interdisciplinary Physics: Nonlinear Dynamics, Biological Physics,
Quantum Electronics, etc.
9. Forum on scientific collaboration among Asia Pacific regions.

CALL FOR ABSTRACTS AND PAPERS
A Conference booklet including abstracts will be distributed to the
participants at the conference.  Abstracts are accepted by email
submission in Microsoft Word or LaTeX format, to
appc2k@phys.sinica.edu.tw or by conventional mail only.

Due to the schedule constraint, only a limited number of contributed
abstracts will be selected for oral presentations by the Program
Committee.  Others will be presented in poster sessions.  Acceptance
notices will be mailed out before May 15, 2000.

ABSTRACT FORMAT
Abstracts should be informative and brief (up to 200 words).  It should
also be camera/scanner ready.  Laser printers of at least 300 dpi are
strongly recommended.  The authors are requested to prepare the
abstracts according to the sample on our web site.  The whole abstract
must be typed in one column of the size: 13cm (wide) by 9cm (high).

PROCEEDINGS
The Proceedings of the Conference will be published by World Scientific
Publishing Co Pte Ltd., (http://www.wspc.com.sg).  Accepted abstracts
must be presented in the conference in order to be eligible for
publication.  Authors of accepted contributions will be requested to
provide a complete version of the paper.  Deadline for the manuscripts
is August 7, 2000.
