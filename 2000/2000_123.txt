演講題目 : STIMULATED RADIATIVE ASSOCIATION OF Li AND H IN THE EARLY UNIVERSE
 
演  講  人 :  郭炳宏同學
                    中央天文所
 
演講時間 : 中華民國89年5月11日 （星期四）
                   中午  12時20分 
 
演講地點 :  科四館 S4-914
 
摘        要 :
 
 The enhancement of the rate coefficient for the radiative association of Li and H 
to form LiH arising from stimulated emission by the cosmic background radiation 
field is calculated. It is shown that the effect on the fractional abundance of 
LiH in the early universe for redshifts z between 1000 and 10 is small.
