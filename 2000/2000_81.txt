            Colloquium (2:00 pm)

            SPEAKER: Prof. Yoshihiro Osamura 
                     (Rikkyo University)
	    TITLE:   "Molecular Orbital Study of the 
	              Formation Rates of Interstellar Molecules"
	              
	    ABSTRACT: 
	    The formation reactions of the various carbon chain molecules in 
 interstellar space have been studied in terms of ab initio molecular orbital 
methods.  The potential energy surfaces of the ion-molecule reactions and
neutral-neut ral reactions reveal that the cyanopolyyne species are mostly
produced by the neutral-neutral reactions C2nH2 + CN.   The formation
mechanism of the iso mers of cyanoacetylene is also discussed.
