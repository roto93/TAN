This Symposium will focus on the very latest VLBI results, including the
most recent  scientific and technical developments. The meeting will have
an informal character with posters and with oral contributions of app. 15
minutes length. We very much encourage contributions from young
astrononomers and students. The EVN Users Meeting will be held at the end
of the conference.

The list of invited speakers will include:

A. Pedlar      	"Starburst Galaxies"
J.-F. Lestrade  "VLBI Astrometry"
G. Elgered     	"VLBI in the service of Geodecy"
C. Fanti       	"Young Radio Sources"
D. Gabuzda     	"Polarization Measurements in VLBI"
H. Hirabayashi 	"Results from VSOP"
T. Krichbaum   	"Millimetre VLBI"
A. Whitney     	"Technical Developments in VLBI" (not yet confirmed)

We are in contact with Elsevier Science and hope to publish the proceedings
in an issue of "New Astronomy Reviews".
