-----------------First Circular ------------------------------------------
We would like to invite you to participate in
 
1st Korean Astrophysics Workshop on 
"Numerical Methods for Astrophysical Flows"
June 26-29, 2001,  Pusan, Korea 

Venue: In-Duk Hall, Pusan National University, Pusan, Korea

Goals: The purpose of this workshop is to review basic understandings 
and recent developments in numerical techniques of hydrodynamics, 
magneto-hydrodynamics, relativistic hydrodynamics, relativistic 
magneto-hydrodynamics, and cosmic-ray modified hydrodynamics in
Astrophysical flows.  They will provide an introduction as well as 
the most up-to-date developments in numerical methods for graduate
students, post-docs and researchers who work or plan to work in these
subjects. It is planned that the codes introduced in the lectures will
be posted in the web page so that others in the field of astrophysics
can retrieve the codes for their research.
