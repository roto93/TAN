The B.M. Birla Science Centre is one of the most prestigious institutions 
in India for the dissemination of science. It regularly organises 
International Symposia, Workshops and Lectures. As many as 16 Nobel 
Laureates and a large number of scientists of the same calibre have 
already participated in these activities.

Keeping in view the Millennium Year, the above Symposium will focus on 
the theme "New Directions in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics".

Eminent scientists from all over the world are expected to participate.

Deadlines:

1.  Abstract (maximum 300 words) - 30th August 2000
2.   Registration (with fees and on a form available on request)
					 - 30th September 2000
3.   Completed paper (as per the format to be given) - 30th November 2000

The registration fees is  US $200/- which includes the Conference folder, 
lunches, Conference dinner, local transport and the cultural programme.

It is expected that the Proceedings will be published.
