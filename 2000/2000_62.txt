  Please be notified that the third Chinese-German Medium Physics
Symposium will be held on March 3 and March 4, 2000 at Robert R. Poe
Auditorium inside the NTU campus. The title is "The Structure of the
Nucleon as from Chiral Perturbation Theory to QCD Sum Rules".

  This is a joint meeting with "Vector Meson Production Workshop"
of Academia Sinica, also in conjunction with the 16th International
Conference on Few Body Problem in Physics, to be held by Dept. of Physics,
NTU, from March 6 to March 10, 2000.

  The list of invited speakers includes:
