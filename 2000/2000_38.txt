Dear Colleagues:

     The Spring School 2000 is the 14th Spring School on Particles 
and Fields, which will be held at National Tsing Hua University from 
March 22 to 25, 2000.  The NCTS Topical Program on ``From Higgs To
Supersymmetry'' will take this gathering opportunity to hold a
miniworkshop for various researchers to report their progress. The
workshop will be held from March 21 to 22, 2000 at NCTS in NTHU.  
If you would like to attend the school and/or workshop, please 
fill out the registration form attached.  The deadline of registration 
is Feb 25. If you have new result or work in progress you wish to 
present at the mini-workshop, please also send your request to

Phenomenology Workshop(March 21-22):
The workshop is intended as a continuation of the current topical 
program: "From Higgs to SUSY" at National Center for Theoretical
Sciences, and will focus on new physics beyond the standard model. 
It will be held on March 21-22, 2000 at Lecture Room of NCTS in NTHU.    
It will be chaired by Prof. J.N. Ng (TRIUMF & NCTS).
