MECHANICAL ENGINEER

Australia Telescope National Facility
Paul Wild Observatory
Narrabri  NSW

$53K - $72K + Superannuation


The Australia Telescope National Facility (ATNF) is the premier radio
astronomical facility in Australia and operates the Compact Array at the
Paul Wild Observatory near Narrabri, the 22m Mopra Radiotelescope near
Coonabarabran and the Parkes Radiotelescope, all in NSW.  The Compact Array
(6 antennas, each 22m in diameter, on a 3km rail track) is currently
undergoing a major upgrade involving civil, mechanical, electronic and
software engineering developments.

The successful applicant will be responsible for the mechanical maintenance
of the Compact Array and Mopra antennas, for managing the team of technical
and trades staff who provide antenna and site engineering services, and
would be expected to contribute to the upgrade work. The observatory
operates 24 hours every day of the year with periods scheduled for
maintenance and development.  Staff members are often required to work out 
of normal
working hours.  Duties will include:

·       monitoring and developing the long term maintenance schedule for
mechanical components of the antennas  and related systems;
·       overseeing mechanical/civil antenna and site works;
·       managing contracted works;
·       contributing to the ongoing assessment and development of all 
aspects of
  the Observatory's mechanical and civil infrastructure, including the
mechanical aspects of antenna optics;
·       on occasions participating in relevant activities at Parkes or the 
ATNF
headquarters in Sydney.

A degree or equivalent in mechanical engineering and several years’
experience is essential. The successful applicant will have demonstrated
project management and leadership experience and will need good written and
oral communication skills.  The ability to climb and work on the telescope
structures and a current driver's license are also required.

This position is for an indefinite period.  The successful applicant will be
eligible for Australian Government superannuation and a district allowance
of up to $1200 per annum.  Where appropriate, relocation expenses will be
provided as will assistance with accommodation for a period of up to 18
months.

Further information about the position can be obtained by contacting Dr
David McConnell, (02) 6790 4000 (or email David.McConnell@atnf.csiro.au).  A
copy of the duty statement and selection criteria can be obtained from Miss
Kylee Forbes on (02) 6790 4024.

Applications should include a curriculum vitae, and the names and addresses
(preferably email) of three referees.  They should be addressed to Dr David
McConnell, Australia Telescope National Facility, Locked Bag 194, Narrabri
NSW 2390 or by email to David.McConnell@atnf.csiro.au and should arrive by
the deadline of 30 June 2000.
