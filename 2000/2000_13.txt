Dear Collegues:
Attached please find the announcement of a new class ‘high energy
physics in the interstellar medium and our solar system’ to be taught at
National Taiwan University, Department of Physics, starting February 21,
2000 (timing and location see http://www.ntu.edu.tw). Please circulate
this to prospective students in the fields of chemistry, physics,
astronomy, astrophysics, atmospheric sciences, and chemical engineering.
This course addresses basic physical processes of high energy physics in
the interstellar medium and helps to understand elementary mechanism in
the interaction of particles and radiation with matter in
extraterrestrial environments. This framework gives the student the
capability to unravel the formation of molecules in the interstellar
medium and our solar system. This understanding of the chemical
processes is a must for every astronomer/astophysicist to get a
comprehensive and systematic understanding of the wealth of astronomical
data and the origin of molecular line surveys.
thanks
ralf kaiser
