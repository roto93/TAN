11th International Symposium on Space Terahertz Technology
    FINAL CALL FOR PAPERS: Abstracts Due 2/7/2000
      The University of Michigan, Ann Arbor
                 May 1-3, 2000
_______________________________________________________________

The Symposium on Space Terahertz Technology features the latest 
research from the U.S.A. and abroad pertaining to the detection, 
generation, and use of terahertz radiation for astronomy, solar 
system studies, remost sensing of Earth's atmosphere, and other 
applications.  Topics of interest for the 11th International 
Symposium include:

	Schottky diode mixer and multiplier technology
	SIS mixer technology
	Hot Electron Bolometers
	Antennas, coupling structures and other passive circuits
	Overviews of space based and astrophysics THz applications
	Direct detection techniques
	Pulsed THz technology
	Non-space applications such as remote senging
	Other topics of interest to the THz community

Authors wishing to submit a papers should submit a one to two-page
abstract.  The abstract should emphasize the new and unique features
of the research.  The deadline for receipt of abstracts is January 31,
2000.  Notification of acceptance will be sent by email by February
29, 2000, along with instructions for the camera-ready Proceedings
papers.  Final papers will be due May 1, 2000.  Abstracts should
include a cover page giving the name, address, phone and fax numbers,
and email address of the correspondence author.  Please email
abstracts in a Word, Framemaker, LaTex, postscript or PDF file, to:
