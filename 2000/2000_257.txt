 Outlines
----------
  

 1.Elements of molecular biology 
   Some basic knowledge of molecular biology and genetics will be
   introduced.
 
 2.Sequence similarity and divergence 
   Models of DNA sequence evolution will be explained and methods for
   estimating evolutionary distances between sequences will be presented.
   
 3.Sequence alignment 
   Basic principles of sequence alignment will be introduced.
   Various methods of sequence alignment will be presented. 
   Examples of application will be included.

 4.Introduction to phylogenetic reconstruction 
   Basic concepts and terminologies of phylogenetic reconstruction will
   be introduced.    
   Methods of tree reconstructions will be presented.
   Some examples will be included.
   
 5.Statistical tests of molecular phylogenies 
   Some statistical aspects of phylogenetic tests will be introduced. 
   Some methods for testing molecular phylogenies will be explained.
