The 5th European VLBI Network Symposium on New Developments in 
VLBI  Science and Technology and the 2000 EVN Users Meeting

are to be held at

ONSALA SPACE OBSERVATORY
Chalmers Centre for Astrophysics and Space Science
Gothenburg/Onsala - Sweden
June 29-July 1, 2000

This Symposium will focus on the very latest VLBI results, including the
most recent scientific and technical developments. The meeting will 
have an informal character with oral contributions of app. 20 minutes 
length. We hope to publish a set of Proceedings. We very much 
encourage contributions from young astrononomers and students.

There will also be a Users Meeting scheduled to cover all issues
raised by users in connection with the current status and development 
of VLBI in Europe. People with views on the EVN and its future direction 
are encouraged to attend. The views of new EVN users are most welcome.

Partial financial support is available for a limited number of young
astronomers through the EU infrastructure Co-operation Network in 
Radio Astronomy. Applications should be sent to:

Dr Leonid Gurvits
Secretary ICN in Radio Astronomy
Joint Institute for VLBI in Europe
Postbus 2, 7990 AA Dwingeloo
The Netherlands

If you would like to attend the symposium, please register your interest by
e-mailing the local organizing committee at "evn@oso.chalmers.se"
and we will then place you on  the Symposium Mailing List and in due 
course send you more information about registration, accommodation, 
programme and so on. The registration deadline is April 14th, 2000.

For further information you can  also visit our website (available in 
approximately one week) at http://www.oso.chalmers.se/evnsymposium.
