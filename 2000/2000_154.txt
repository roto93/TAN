            Colloquium (2:00 pm)

            SPEAKER: Prof. Don Kurtz 
                     (Cape Town University)
	    TITLE:   "The Music of the Spheres: Nonradial Pulsation in 
	              three classes of variable stars discovered in 
	              South Africa, and asteroseimological results from them"
	    
	    ABSTRACT:
This talk will introduce nonradial stellar pulsation - what it looks
like, what it is, and how stars are like giant musical instruments. Most
importantly, it will show how seismology of stars, asteroseismology, can
be used to probe their otherwise-inaccessible interiors. Examples shown
will be the rapidly oscillating Ap stars, gamma Doradus stars and the EC
14026 subdwarf B stars - all discovered by astronomers currently working 
in South Africa.
