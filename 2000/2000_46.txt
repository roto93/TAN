**********AUSTRALIA TELESCOPE NATIONAL FACILITY ***********

Applications for observing time are now invited for the 2000MAYT term 
for the following telescopes:

Compact Array, Narrabri 
Parkes telescope, Parkes 
Mopra telescope, Coonabarabran 
VLBI observations 

*********************************************************************** 
CLOSING DATE for applications is 15th February 2000 
For 5 month Observing term MAY 1st to SEPTEMBER 30th 2000 
************************************************************************

Users are advised that to facilitate scheduling during the Sydney Olympics the 
2000MAYT term has been extended by one extra month to cover a five month 
period.

For the Compact Array, proposers should note that the term will start in 
the 750D configuration. The other configurations offered for the term are the 
6A, 6B, 1.5A, 750A & 375 arrays. During the term there will be a maintenance 
period of approximately three weeks.

Application forms, telescope documentation and full information on how to 
apply for ATNF telescope time are available on the web at the address 
http://www.atnf.csiro.au/observers/

Please note that the scientific case for telescope applications should not 
exceed a total of 3 pages (including figures). A font size of at least 10 point

should be used.
