Important Message from The University of Chicago Press



The University of Chicago Press offices will be closed on 
Thursday, 4 January, and Friday, 5 January, as the Press moves 
to a new building. The Press will reopen on Monday, 8 January 2001.
The new address is 1427 E. 60th Street, Chicago, IL 60637-2954. 
There will be no telephone number changes.

ONLINE JOURNALS. The online journals (ApJ and AJ) server will be 
unavailable from early morning until early afternoon (CST)
Saturday, 13 January.

PROOFS. Please do not send in proof corrections, faxes, 
email, or any express packages during the week of 1 January 2001.

APJ SUBMISSIONS. The ApJ FTP site (jrnls-ftp.uchicago.edu) will 
be unavailable between 6 pm CST Wednesday, 3 January, and early 
Monday, 8 January. The ApJ Web Peer Review site (mss.uchicago.edu) 
will be unavailable for submissions of new or revised manuscripts
from noon Wednesday, 3 January, until early Monday, 8 January.
Authors, reviewers, and editors will still be able to access
manuscripts already under review until Saturday, 6 January. The
entire Web Peer Review site will be unavailable from noon CST
Saturday, 6 January, until early Monday, 8 January.

DNS CHANGES. In addition to moving to a new building, we are 
also moving to a new subnet. The DNS changes will take effect 
at 12:00 am Sunday, 7 January. If you have difficulty reaching 
our servers by host name after the move, here are the new IP
addresses:

   jrnls-ftp.uchicago.edu  128.135.181.17 
   mss.uchicago.edu        128.135.181.18

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION. If there are any last-minute changes, 
we will post information at our web site

   http://www.journals.uchicago.edu

and at the AAS website
