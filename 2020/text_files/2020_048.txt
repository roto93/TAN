ALMA Cycle 8 Call for Proposals is Now OPEN!
=09
The ALMA Director, on behalf of the Joint ALMA Observatory and the =
partner organizations in East Asia, Europe, and North America, is =
pleased to announce that the ALMA Cycle 8 Call for Proposals for =
scientific observations is now OPEN!

ALMA Cycle 8 is currently scheduled for observations from October 2020 =
to September 2021. Users of any nationality or affiliation are invited =
to submit proposals before the deadline of 15:00 UT on Wednesday 15 =
April 2020.

ALMA is carefully monitoring the situation regarding the  COVID-19 =
pandemic and the evolving global situation and is considering the =
possibility of needing to delay the proposal deadline which is currently =
set for 15:00 UT on Wednesday 15 April 2020.  Updates will be posted to =
the ALMA Science Portal on a weekly basis at http://almascience.org.  =
The first update will be posted on Tuesday, 24 March 2020.  For =
additional questions regarding the Cycle 8 Call for Proposals, contact =
the ALMA Helpdesk at http://help.almascience.org.

For more information, please see =
https://almascience.nao.ac.jp/proposing/call-for-proposals
