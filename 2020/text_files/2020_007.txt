First Announcement

International Astronomical Symposium: What A Wonderful World

To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Delta Education Foundation that is
the sponsor of the Delta Young Astronomer Award, a topical symposium will
be held between July 21 and 23 at National Central University in Taiwan.
Invited talks will be given by the following Delta award winners to depict
the wonders of the Universe and our Solar System:

Eva Grebel, University of Heidelberg
Mathew Holman, Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics
Nissim Kanekar, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research
Lisa Kewley, Australian National University
Chao-Lin Kuo, Stanford University
Jifeng Liu, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences
Christopher Reynolds, Cambridge University
Stephen Smartt, Queen's University, Belfast
Masaomi Tanaka, University of Tohoku
Jean-Baptiste Vincent, German Aerospace Center
