The University of Tokyo ICRR Postdoctoral Research Fellow

We invite applications for the University of Tokyo ICRR Postdoctral
Research fellowship
at the University of Tokyo, Kashiwa Campus to begin April, 1, 2013.
The fellows will
work in a field of astrophysics or particle physics that includes
observational
cosmology/galaxy formation in any wavelengths, theoretical cosmology,
neutrino/dark matter experiments, high energy cosmic ray/gamma ray
observations,
and gravitational wave experiments. The successful applicant must have
completed
the Ph.D. requirements before assuming the fellowship. The stipend is
326,535 JPY per month. A health insurance and commutation allowance
will be supported. Fellowships are awarded for one year and may
be renewed for one additional year (two years in total). By the end of the
appointment, fellows can apply for this fellowship, but the total
appointment period
cannot exceed five consecutive years. The interview is scheduled on
Jan. 12, 2013
for short-listed applicants.

The application should include 1) a curriculum vitae, 2) a brief essay
describing
the applicant's past research (within 2 pages), 3) a brief essay describing
the applicant's research plan (within 2 pages), 4) bibliography and
reprints of applicant's major papers (within 3 papers),
and 5) a name of faculty contact at ICRR the University of Tokyo
(see http://www.icrr.u-tokyo.ac.jp/about/group_eng.html).
These materials must be submitted via e-mail or mail (addresses shown below)
that should arrive by 17:00 on
Dec. 21, 2012 (JST).
In addition, applicants should arrange for two letters of reference
to be e-mailed to kajita@icrr.u-tokyo.ac.jp by the deadline.
E-mail inquiries may be sent to director Takaaki Kajita.
