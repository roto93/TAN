***** Announcement of SOKENDAI Asian Winter School *****

The Graduate
University for Advanced Studies (Sokendai) will give a series
of lectures and
training course for Asian students and young scientists.
Main topic of this year
at NAOJ is " observations of exoplanets".
We expect many participants who are
interested in exoplanets or
extrasolar planets.

[2]

*Dates
December 3-5, 2012

*Place
National Astronomical Observatory of
Japan
Mitaka Campus
http://www.nao.ac.jp/E/access/

*Eligibility
Undergraduate students (high priority), graduate students, and
young researchers
from universities or research institutions of Asian
countries.

*Maximum number of participants
15 (first-come-first-served
basis)

*How to apply
Fill application form online and send a reference
letter
from faculty of university or research institution.

*Financial
supports
We will provide participants' travel fee and accommodation
fee.
