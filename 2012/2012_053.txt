Dear colleagues,

	This is to remind you that the early registration deadline for ASROC2012 is approaching in three days!

	The ASROC2012 (2012 Scientific Assembly of the Astronomical Society of the Republic of China) is to be held at National Taitung University on May 25-27, 2012, and you are cordially invited to participate.

	Given the remote location of the meeting, the organizing committee will help making group reservations for the round-trip train tickets between Taipei and Taitung. The Taiwan Railways requires group reservations to be made two months prior to the trip. Thus, if you would like to take advantage of our service, your cooperation to register before March 23 (Friday) is very important.

	Information about the lodging and half-day tour is available now on the meeting website, and the abstract submission interface will be online in a day or two. The abstract submission deadline is April 27, about another month from now.

	For the 2nd circular and more detailed information, please visit the ASROC2012 meeting website at
       http://www.asroc.org.tw/asroc2012/index.php

	Thank you for your attention, and we look forward to seeing you at the meeting!

Sheng-Yuan Liu, on behalf of the ASROC2012 LOC
