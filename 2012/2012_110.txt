First Circular
APCTP Workshop on Astrophysics: Magnetic Fields in Astrophysics Nov. 19
(Mon) -- Nov. 22 (Thu), 2012, APCTP in POSTECH, Pohang, KOREA
http://www.apctp.org/plan.php/**kj2012<http://www.apctp.org/plan.php/kj2012>
==============================**==============================

APCTP Workshop on Astrophysics 2012 will take place from November 19 (Mon)
to November 22 (Thu) at Asia Pacific Center for Theoretical Physics (APCTP)
within Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), Pohang, KOREA.

Researchers and students from Asia-Pacific regions including APCTP member
countries will present and discuss their recent works and review the
current status of research in following fields: Magnetic fields in Galactic
center, Magnetic fields around black holes and neutron stars, Magnetic
fields in accretion disks, Magnetic fields in Sun and stars, Magnetic
fields in star formation, Magnetic fields in jets, supernovae, GRBs,
Magnetic fields in interstellar medium, Magnetic fields in intergalactic
medium, MHD Simulations, Primordial magnetic fields, and Earth
Magnetosphere.
The program consists of invited talks, contributed talks, and poster
papers. We call for contributed talks and posters. Postdocs and graduate
students are especially welcomed.
